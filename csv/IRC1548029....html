Department Description
Oracle's Netra Mechanical Group is responsible for the mechanical and thermal design of  highly scalable high quality Netra systems and is the center of excellence for Networking, Security, and Fabrics. Our engineers design these systems utilizing both JDM and Internal/EM models to provide Oracle customers with the variety they demand for their unique computing needs.  While the core of our group is mechanical engineering R&D, its collaboration with marketing, operations, services, and other key functional partners is indispensable to its ability to bring top-notch products to market successfully and in a competitive time frame.  The majority of  Netra Mechanical Group employees are located in the California Bay Area and in Burlington, MA.
Brief Description
Evaluates reliability of materials, properties and techniques used in production; plans, designs and develops electronic parts, components, integrated circuitry, mechanical systems, equipment and packaging, optical systems and/or DSP systems.
Detailed Description
Responsible for designing, developing, modifying and evaluating electronic, electro-mechanical or mechanical components, assemblies or integrated circuitry for hardware systems for the external market. Includes new design, as well as modification activities that results in significant product enhancement. Activities encompass design, analysis, testing and process development using engineering principles and methods. Technical disciplines may include electrical and logical design of printed circuit boards or integrated circuits; mechanical design of electronics enclosures or integrated circuit packaging; and embedded software/firmware design.
Job Requirements
Work is non-routine and very complex, involving the application of advanced technical/business skills in area of specialization. Leading contributor individually and as a team member, providing direction and mentoring to others. BS or MS degree or equivalent experience relevant to functional area. 7 years of engineering or related experience.
Additional Details
LOCATION OF POSITION:  Santa Clara Campus (ONLY)

ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:

Lead Mechanical engineer to drive projects within a product design team responsible for providing
mechanical engineering and design support to ATCA, Telco server, and IB/Networking product teams. This
individual will be a senior technical lead responsible for driving and executing the mechanical design of new
development and sustaining engineering projects. The position requires someone who demonstrates ability
to provide technical advice, leadership, and direction to junior engineers. Essential skills include the ability to
independently develop solutions to multiple complex mechanical design problems while negotiating cross-functional
and cross-disciplinary requirements (i.e., EMI vs. thermal, Design for manufacturing/service vs.
cost) into manufacturable solutions.

Key responsibilities of this position include the ability to manage projects and direct the activity of other
product design professionals. In addition, the requires analysis and optimization of designs for thermal,
structural, shock/vibration, and acoustics. Other responsibilities include, but are not limited to, integration of
cooling solutions, PCBA mechanical specification and interconnect design, ASIC/heatsink attach and
representing product design interests and requirements while interacting with cross-functional team
members, vendors, and ODM/JDM partners.

MARGINAL FUNCTIONS:

* Design and development of mechanical parts and assemblies (sheet metal,plastic and machined) while
taking into consideration design for EMI, Manufacturing/serviceability, safety, industrial design, and cost.
* Representing mechanical engineering function to product teams
* Bill of Materials development
* Supporting bring-up of hard tooling as required.
