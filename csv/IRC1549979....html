Job Title Principal Consultant
Location BOG
Organization Name Consulting
Department Description

Lead in the early and accurate definition of an application based solution, addressing the client’s business and system requirements.

Scope

  • Reports to Consulting Practice Manager and works as part of a practice team

  • Works within a designated Oracle product or application practice area

  • Works closely with Client Managers and other Practice members

  • Interacts with and works alongside the Client team, at customer site as/when required

  • May lead a team depending on the situation

Responsibilities

  • Assist the relevant teams to describe/explain Oracle’s approach and solutions capabilities

  • Presenting application solutions to the relevant clients

  • Gathering requirements information for qualified opportunities with designated clients

  • Reviewing invitations to tender and advising in the context of the practice area

  • Working with the client to understand data requirements, functionality and business processes

  • Designing, developing and documenting a solutions footprint and application architecture in accordance with the client’s requirements

  • Building and maintaining relationships at the relevant levels in the client’s organization

  • Escalating client issues to the relevant contact in Oracle

  • Assisting in the development of a comprehensive implementation plan

  • Get agreement of implementation plan with the client

  • Work with Delivery Management to draw up a comprehensive project plan

  • Organizing and attending Oracle internal as well as client meetings, as required

  • Training client staff as required by the project plan

  • Deploying the Implementation Methodology as required

  • Representing Oracle at internal and external events in the context of the specialist application area

 Accountabilities

  • Act as the design authority and owner for the application configuration

  • Translate customer requirements into deliverable products and services

  • Represent Oracle professionally to clients and other third parties

  • Follow and maintain the Project Plan

  • Proactively maintain knowledge of Oracle’s products/solutions

Brief Description
An experienced consulting professional who has an understanding of solutions, business processes, or technology designs within a product/technology family.
Detailed Description
Provide engagement delivery services both as an individual and as a team member. Has subject matter knowledge of relevant implementation responsibility. Poss esses database, core applications and web technology knowledge. Has specific ex perience in implementing Oracle solutions and in systems integrations.
