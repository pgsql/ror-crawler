Job Title Telesales Business Development Representative II
Location GURGAON2
Organization Name Insight Global Scalability Team (IBU)
Department Description

Industries Business Unit (IBU) brings industry domain expertise into every customer and partner interaction. Oracle Insight is a Sales Driven Program within IBU focused on driving license revenue. Insight helps articulate Oracle’s unique business value proposition to customer executives

This program helps sales staff :

  • to gain smart access to newer accounts
  • to elevate the conversation to higher levels, and
  • to accelerate the decision making process and help close deals faster
Brief Description
Order fulfillment and selling of products and services in high volume, low value, short cycle transactions using telephone sales.
Detailed Description
As a Telesales Business Development Representative you will act as the first contact for many prospects in Oracle business with inbound telemarketing calls. Qualifies Oracle prospects and produce quality leads for the Oracle Sales Force. Performs database and record keeping activities.
Job Requirements
Ability to demonstrate time management, telesales skills and knowledge of Oracle products and services (training provided). Goal oriented individual with superior communication skills. Ability to complete individual goals as well as work in a team environment. Demonstrated ability to communicate using technical concepts. Working knowledge of Oracle products preferred. Desire for a long-term career in sales preferred. Two years relevant business experience preferred.
Additional Details

Specific Responsibilities:

  • Execution of qualitative & financial analyses in support of individual Oracle sales cycles
  • Creation of high quality presentations of increasing complexity in support of sales cycles
  • Effectively interact with global sales teams, and support development of turnkey deliverables with elements of financial analysis, interpretation and story boarding

Performance Metrics:

  • Throughput  targets and quality standards for deliverables
  • Sales team satisfaction, based on requester surveys

Education & Experience:

  • MBA from well regarded university
  • 2- 4 years of relevant work experience in good KPO organizations, market/ equity research companies
  • Financial analysis capabilities

Competencies:

  • Good understanding of financial statements & terms, their implications, comparative financial analysis
  • Strong oral and written communication skills
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office applications

Additional Details:

  • Contrary to the Job title, this role does NOT involve tele-sales
  • It does NOT involve working in shifts
  • It involves doing industry & business research to support Oracle sales cycles
