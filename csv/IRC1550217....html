Job Title Telesales Business Development Representative II
Location MINNEAPOLIS
Organization Name Oracle Direct
Department Description

The business development consultant (BDC) is a strategic member of the sales team that is responsible for identifying and nurturing new sales opportunities.  In conjunction with the various members of the virtual sales team, the BDC is responsible for developing a comprehensive and disciplined strategy for penetrating new accounts or divisions of existing accounts that have a limited Oracle presence. As such, the BDC will develop a strong understanding of their territory’s dynamics including understanding the major industry trends, the historical sales patterns and current business trends, identifying the gaps in a customer’s product portfolio and developing a strategy to fill those gaps. The BDC then executes that strategy by proactively reaching out to prospects and sharing the value that Oracle can bring to their business.  When calling on decision makers, the BDC will be able to anticipate and understand the company’s business challenges and translate how Oracle can address those challenges.  Since the BDC will not have a prior relationship with these executives, they should be able to quickly establish credibility and demonstrate in words how they can add value to the business.  The goal is to uncover quality sales opportunities where Oracle has a strong chance of winning the business. 


Brief Description
Order fulfillment and selling of products and services in high volume, low value, short cycle transactions using telephone sales.
Detailed Description
As a Telesales Business Development Representative you will act as the first contact for many prospects in Oracle business with inbound telemarketing calls. Qualifies Oracle prospects and produce quality leads for the Oracle Sales Force. Performs database and record keeping activities.
Job Requirements
Ability to demonstrate time management, telesales skills and knowledge of Oracle products and services (training provided). Goal oriented individual with superior communication skills. Ability to complete individual goals as well as work in a team environment. Demonstrated ability to communicate using technical concepts. Working knowledge of Oracle products preferred. Desire for a long-term career in sales preferred. Two years relevant business experience preferred.
Additional Details

This is a challenging role that will test one’s strategic thinking capabilities, one’s ability to remain focused and follow through on plans despite multiple distractions, as well as one’s perseverance in forging new relationships in theface of rejection.  The BDC will be expected to constantly deliver more – more qualified opportunities, higher revenue conversions and more innovative approaches.  Because one is typically going into new territories, one should expect little support in the way of contacts or leads.  Instead, Oracle will provide the tools, raw data, training and people resources to build a successful demand generation business. 

The BDC typically covers several sales regions that map to one or more field sales managers and inside sales reps.  Even though the BDC may have the least amount of time covering the territory, they will nonetheless be expected to bring new ideas and insights to the table on how to grow the overall business.  The more successful one is in delivering value and revenue to the sales territory , the more resources will become available for the BDC to grow their business.  Moreover, the skills, contacts and trackrecord that are developed in this role will play an important role in enhancing one’s career potential within Oracle.
