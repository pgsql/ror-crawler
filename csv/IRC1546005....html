Department Description

Oracle’s AutoVue suite of enterprise visualization solutions provides native document viewing, markup, and real-time collaboration capabilities that enable Web-based document review on hundreds of document types without compromising security or precision. In business for the last 20 years and acquired by Oracle in 2007, AutoVue provides a unique technology to view and annotate documents created from various applications ranging from PDF and Word to the most complex 2D and 3D designs. Enabling collaboration on any documents, it is mission critical to world leading companies around the globe that build, manufacture and maintain complex products or assets such as aircrafts, nuclear plants and electronic components,…

The AutoVue documentation team is responsible for development of administrative, installation, API and other technical documentation for the AutoVue line of products and AutoVue’s pre-integrated solutions for market-leading ECM and PLM systems (UCM, Dassault-ENOVIA, EMC-Documentum,…). As part of the documentation team, you will plan, create, design and develop documentation deliverables for the AutoVue suite of products. 

The Documentation team will be responsible for creation and maintenance of over 50 documents across more than 10 products.
Brief Description

Creates, develops, plans, writes and edits operational, instructional, maintenance, test or user manuals for paper, multimedia or web-based publications. Contributes to the timely design, production and delivery/completion of product documentation and document sets.

Créer, développer, planifier, rédiger et modifier les manuels d’exploitation, d’instruction, de maintenance, d’essai ou d’utilisation pour les publications sur support papier, multimédia ou Web. Participer à la conception, à la production et à la livraison/finalisation en temps opportun de la documentation sur les produits.
Detailed Description

As a member of the technical writing division, you will design, create and deliver whole product documentation sets and/or related courseware. Edit, organize and direct the work activities of other technical writers and staff. Plan the format, arrange the layout of publications and edit work for conformance to publication standards and specifications.

Concevoir, créer et produire, à titre de membre de la division de rédaction technique, les didacticiels ou les documents relatifs à des produits. Organiser et diriger les activités de travail du personnel et des autres rédacteurs techniques. Prévoir le format, préparer la mise en page des publications et modifier le travail afin qu’il soit conforme aux normes et aux spécifications de publication.
