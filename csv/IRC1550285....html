Department Description
FusionTerritory Management is a keyproduct in Fusion CRM product suite. It provides sales managers orterritoryadministrators a tool to create optimized territories defined in termsof geographic ormarket segments, product or product lines, size of customer, or byspecificcustomers or prospects.These territories serveas a basis for forecasting, quota, compensation, and analysis of sales performance and help provide a higherlevel of service. Optimized territories willprovide representativesmore time to follow-up on new business opportunities by reducing traveltime,creating more revenue per coverage area.FusionTerritory Management is beingbuilt on Oracle Fusion middleware technologies, which includes ADF, ADFUI, SOA,Enterprise Scheduler, Essbase and BI.
