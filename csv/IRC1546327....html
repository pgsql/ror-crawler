Position Overview:
 
The Commodity Program Manager will be part of the department that manages the suppliers for custom Power Supply Units and SPARC microprocessors.  This position's role is to develop, set up procurement and logistics processes for microprocessors with foundry businesses.  Set and drive strategies to manage critical material availability throughout the supply chain.  Experience in MRP, procurement process in fabless model, forecasting, inventory control and warehousing.  Strong Supply Chain / Logistics and  program management skills are essentials. Track record of working cross-functionally to achieve/exceed business goals.  

Responsibilities:
-  Develop and set up procurement, inventory, warehousing  processes with foundry and back end assembly supplier.
- Set and drive strategies to manage critical material availability throughout the supply chain during all phases of product life cycle.
- Drive strategic initiatives internally and externally to meet business requirements for a core Oracle commodity
 - Develop and manage best practices for all aspects of the core commodity supplier management processes.
