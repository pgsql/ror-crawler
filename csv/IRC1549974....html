Job Title Product Mgmt/Strategy Director-ProdDev
Location BANGALORE
Organization Name FS GBU Product Marketing
Department Description

 The Financial Services GBU is responsible for driving industry applications value propositions, messaging, collateral and development plans for Banking, Wealth Management and Capital Markets. 

For our Banking industry-segment, we are investing in developing and enhancing our Oracle FLEXCUBE and Next Generation Offerings. 

Brief Description
Manage a team that acts as the central resource and driving force for the design, process, manufacturing, test, quality and marketing of product(s) as they move from conception to distribution. Organize interdepartmental activities ensuring completion of the project/product on schedule and within budget.
Detailed Description
As a Director in the product development division, you will lead a team that defines product specifications and or strategy. Gather and analyze information to define product specifications and review design specifications. Envision and communicate product strategy and functionality. Initiate and foster relationships with other groups. Review product documentation and collateral. Ensure successful product releases based on corporate priorities.
Job Requirements
Assists in the development of short, medium, and long term plans to achieve strategic objectives. Regularly interacts across functional areas with senior management or executives to ensure unit objectives are met. The ability to influence thinking or gain acceptance of others in sensitive situations is important. Demonstrated leadership and people management skills. Strong communication skills, analytical skills, thorough understanding of product development and product management. Preferred Qualifications: BS or MS degree or equivalent experience relevant to functional area. 8 of software engineering or related experience preferred.
Additional Details

As a member of the product management team you’ll be responsible for drving the outbound product management team in Bangalore.  This entails working with the inbound product management team, FS IBU, marketing, sales and pre-sales members to develop value propositions, identify opportunities, help drive the products into the market and ensure that the materials are up to the standards of Oracle.

 

You will be directly responsible for creating value propositions, product presentations, data sheets, whitepapers, FAQs, and content for Oracle.com.  You will also participate in analyst briefings and drive customer facing activities such as a Customer Advisory Board (CAB) for our revenue management and billing users.

 

As a member of the FS-GBU Product Management team, you will:

  • Develop value propositions around our product offerings
  • Work with the IBU, Sales and Pre-Sales to tailor the value proposition to key prospects
  • Work with the inbound Product Management team to prioritize enhancements for product releases.
  • Develop product collateral (presentations, datasheets, FAQ, whitepapers, content for Oracle.com), demos and training, execute Customer Advisory Boards with customer’s community and participate in analyst presentations.
  • Apply domain expertise in the area of revenue management, packaging/bundling, billing and collections and product management to deliver a best-in-class banking solution targeted at Tier 1/Tier 2 markets. 
  • Collaborate with other product management team members, engineering, marketing and sales teams to identify, define and validate product enhancements, integration touch-points and technology uptakes across Oracle’s portfolio of solutions.
  • Collaborate with inbound product management and industry marketing teams to review BRDs, prioritize product investments in the areas of market awareness, and adoption.
  • Collaborate with Alliances & Channels and Oracle’s Professional Services (consulting) organization to drive customer success.

Customer Success:

Manage strategic customers; understand their key business processes across the value chain to define product enhancements

Actively engage with customers to ensure deployment success

Nurture customers to create case studies, presentations and other marketing content

 

Preferred Skills:

Industry or Product Background:

Strong industry knowledge of banking applications including front office, core banking and originations.

 

Prior experience in a professional a product management role with responsibility for licensed product release planning and value proposition work is preferred. 

  • Business Processes:  A key goal of this role is to define the key business processes needed to ensure end-to-end support of the business processes the application needs to support.
  • Writing/ Messaging Skills:  You will be responsible for developing the core business value propositions for management, marketing, IBU sales and presales..  To do so, you’ll need to be experienced and gifted in succinctly describing the business value of the solution enhancements.     
  • Presentation Skills:  In this role, you will present in front of large groups.  You should be accomplished and comfortable with this job skill.
  • Sales/ Sales Training Skills:  Another key goal of this position is to support the IBU and sales teams in arming them with the ammunition and skills they need to properly position products and close opportunities. Experience in sales, developing solution collateral, white papers or sales training is therefore desirable.
  • Cross-Functional Management and Influencing Skills:  Product Management works with a variety of people across the organization:  Other product managers, Engineering, Corporate/Industry/Product Marketing, Industries Business Unit, Field Sales, Alliances and Channels, Consulting and Training.  In many cases, success in this role mandates the ability to get people in other functions to make timely contributions to a variety of product industry strategy tactics.  As such, the ability to influence and manage across functions is vital.
  • Multi-Tasking and Organization Skills:  Ability to be organized and keep lots of diverse activities moving forward is a key.
  • Leadership Skills:  You will be responsible for working with a diverse group of other product managers and engineers.  The ability to provide leadership while allowing them to exercise their own professional skills is critical.
  • Interpersonal Skills:  One of great aspects of this role is that you will interface with all kinds of people at many different levels.  As such, you’ll need great interpersonal skills to be successful.
  • Product Skills:  In this role, you’ll need to understand and learn the functional details and product architecture of the solutions you’ll be working with. 
  • Ability to Travel:  The FS-GBU has a large employee base in India and Ireland.  This role involves periodic travel to these two locations.  Additionally there may be an occasional need to travel to meet with customers, attend meetings and events and speaking engagements. 
  • Education:  BA/BS recommended; MBA or equivalent experience preferred.
