Job Title Materials Handler A2-Mfg&Dist
Location US-Hillsboro-NW 215th Ave
Organization Name Inventory Services
Department Description
Brief Description
Responsible for shipping, receiving, storing, and distributing materials, parts supplies, and equipment.
Detailed Description
Responsible for receiving, storing, and shipping of inventory material. Assembles kitted inventory. Provides measurements of warehouse processes such as cycle counting. Ensures processes meet or exceed ISO needs. May lift heavy items and may operate a forklift. Assists with warehouse projects as needed.
Job Requirements
Works on assignments that are semi-routine in nature where ability to recognize deviation from standard practice is needed. Normally receives detailed instructions for new assignments. Be computer literate familiar with MS Office Suite. BA/BS degree. 1 - 2 years of experience in high volume warehouse environment. Proficient in shipping processes and specifications both domestically and internationally. Self motivated. Attention to detail.
Additional Details
Works with 3rd party warehousing supplier, providing instruction and direction to them as needed.
