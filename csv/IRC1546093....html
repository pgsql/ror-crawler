Department Description

Oracle SOA is a single platform for all enterprise integration challenges: it provides application-to-application, business-to-business, business process management, enterprise service bus and business activity monitoring. With Oracle SOA, customers can connect disparate information sources to seamlessly exchange data, streamline business processes, and collaborate with their partners.

 The Oracle SOA Integration development team is developing the most complete, J2EE and open standards-based software product for Business-to- Business Integration and Healthcare. Oracle B2B platform provides support for business partner integration using various B2B standards like AS2, ebXML, RosettaNet, EDI and HL7. Oracle B2B provides a comprehensive solution for Trading Partner management, document handling, customization and multi-level security management.
