Job Title Nurse Practitioner - NICU
Department Name NICU
Job Requirements

EDucation/Training/Experience:

  • Current Louisiana RN and Advanced Practice Nurse (APRN) licensure
  • Current  or application for Louisiana Prescriptive Authority
  • Master’s Degree in Nursing with specialization in Neonatal Nurse Practitioner
  • Certification by a national certifying organization, National Credentialing Center (NCC) with renewal every three (3) years as required by certifying body.
  • Current BLS and NRP certification.
Status, Shifts and Hours Worked

Fulltime

Requires Louisiana Board Certified NNP with a minimum of 1 year experience and a minimum of 5 years experience as a Level III NICU nurse.
