Department Description

About the Applications Technology Group:

E-Business Suite Applications Technology Group (also known as ATG) provides the technology framework to develop, manage and integrate E-Business Suite Applications. ATG provides products and solutions around EBS Application Development, Integration, Life cycle Management, and User Management.ATG product suite comprises of products like Oracle Application Framework (OAF), Integrated SOA Gateway, EBS Adapter, EBS Secure Enterprise Search (SES), Application Management Pack for Oracle E-Business Suite (AMP), Application Change Management Pack for Oracle E-Business Suite (ACMP), Rapid Install / Clone (RI/RC), to name a few.

About the Product Management team:

ATG Product Management is the core team within the product development organization that owns the product life cycle right from envisioning the product strategy, defining the product specifications to driving the field enablement and product launch initiatives.ATG Product Managers are involved in both inbound and outbound product management activities. Product Managers for ATG are the experts and advocates for the EBS technology products and infrastructure and are responsible for leveraging these throughout the E-Business Suite application suite.
Brief Description
Lead a team that acts as the central resource and driving force for the design, process, manufacturing, test, quality and marketing of product(s) as they move from conception to distribution. Organize interdepartmental activities ensuring completion of the project/product on schedule and within budget.
Detailed Description
As a member of the product development division, you will specify, design and implement major changes to existing software architecture. Define project needs. Build and execute unit tests and unit test plans. Review integration and regression plans created by QA. Communicate with QA and porting engineering to ensure consistency, testability and portability across products in general.
Job Requirements
Provide leadership and expertise in the development of new products/services/processes, frequently operating at the leading edge of technology. Recommends and justifies major changes to existing products/services/processes. BS or MS degree or equivalent experience relevant to functional area. 8 or more years of software engineering or related experience.
Additional Details

About the Role:

As a product manager, you will work on detailed functional specifications. Assist in release planning and prioritization. Build enhancements (including new product features) and resolve customer issues. Build and execute functional test cases and plans. Review integration and regression test plans created by QA. Interact with QA, DEV, Release and Documentation teams.

As a Product Manager, you will drive all field and customer enablement activities including trainings, marketing events and collateral creation.
