Department Description
Brief Description
As a member of the Consulting organization, you are responsible for ensuring that a quality, integrated software solution is delivered in a timely manner, at budget, and to our client*s satisfaction. This involves working closely with the client to understand and manage their expectations, as well as working closely with Oracle and third-party vendors to ensure delivery.
Detailed Description
Serves in a client facing executive management role. Establishes and communicates the vision for the practice. Plans, organizes, directs and controls the activities and staff that supports the vision. Responsible for business development, operational results, quality of delivery, and cultivation of client references for Oracle.
Job Requirements
Assists in the creation of division strategy and consults with senior management in providing direction to ensure growth and financial success. Bachelor's Degree in Computer Sciences or Business or equivalent. MBA preferred. Balance of advanced technical skills, functional knowledge, client consultative skills and operational management experience. Has 8+ years of consulting services senior management experience with P & L responsibility and 10-15 years consulting or industry experience. Willing to travel regionally with some national/international travel.
