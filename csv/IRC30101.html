Job Title Customer Service Coordinator

Department Description
Brief Description

2 OPENINGS

 

This position is responsible for managing/coaching the CSR's in response to customer issues and complex inquiries and monitoring work queues and distribution of workloads to achieve departmental goals and standards.
Detailed Description
  • Leads/coaches the CSR's in response to customer issues, complex inquiries and difficult situations.

  • Monitors work queues and distribution of workload for adequate coverage, ensuring departmental goals are achieved and maintained. Recommend to supervisor or manager how to address deficiencies.

  • Provides immediate feedback to supervisors and managers of individual CSR performance issues with recommendations for development  in order to attain quality customer service.

  • Performs miscellaneous duties as assigned.

 
Job Requirements
  • Flexibility and ability to work until 9:00 PM EST

  • A high school diploma or GED.  Bachelor's degree preferred. 

  • Three or more year's customer service or call center experience.

  • Dental assisting certificate, 2 years dental office or dental lab experience, or 1 year dental claims adjudication experience preferred.

  • Ability to pass CSR II test.

  • Knowledge of dental terminology and dental claim processing, procedures and guidelines.

  • Bilingual in Spanish preferred. 

  • Strong organizational/time management skills and multi-tasking abilities.

  • Detail-oriented; flexible and creative.

  • Self motivated.

  • Demonstrated ability to maintain confidentiality.

  • Ability to work in a fast paced environment and effectively handle constant interruptions and resolve conflicts.

  • Ability to handle irate callers and escalated calls effectively. 

  • Ability to make effective business recommendations and develop methods and procedures to improve department's effectiveness and efficiency.

  • Demonstrated coaching and training skills.

  • Strong interpersonal and communications skills to work effectively with all levels of staff.

  • Must be PC literate; MSOffice skills (Word, Excel, PowerPoint).

  • Ability to operate standard office equipment (personal computer, photocopy machine, fax machine, etc.).

  • Ability to work at a computer up to 7 hours a day.

  • Regularly required to sit for long periods of time, and occasionally stand and walk.

  • Regularly uses hands to operate computer and other office equipment.

  • Close vision required for computer usage.

  • Occasionally required to stoop, kneel, climb and lift up to 25 pounds.

  • Ability to clearly present written information.

  • Ability to understand and follow work rules and procedures.

  • Ability to make effective presentations.

  • Ability to effectively communicate information to all departments.

  • Interpersonal skills which are conducive to teamwork and collaboration.
