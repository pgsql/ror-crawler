Job Title Software Developer 5
Location BROOMFIELD
Organization Name Oracle On Track Development
Department Description

On Track is a highly innovative collaboration product developed in-house at Oracle.  As a new and rapidly growing business it is sold to customers as a stand-alone social collaboration tool, integrated and embedded with our applications and as a platform for collaborative development.
Brief Description
Design, develop, troubleshoot and debug software programs for databases, applications, tools, networks etc.
Detailed Description
As a member of the software engineering division, you will take an active role in the definition and evolution of standard practices and procedures. Define specifications for significant new projects and specify, design and develop software according to those specifications. You will perform professional software development tasks associated with the developing, designing and debugging of software applications or operating systems.
Job Requirements
Provide leadership and expertise in the development of new products/services/processes, frequently operating at the leading edge of technology. Recommends and justifies major changes to existing products/services/processes. BS or MS degree or equivalent experience relevant to functional area. 8 more years of software engineering or related experience.
Additional Details

As a member of the On Track Solution Architecture team tasked with creating global reference customers, the applicant will have a broad range of business and technical skills.  They will be a highly motivated, self-starter with the ability to quickly become a valuable contributor to the team.  The successful candidate will likely have consulting experience and will be comfortable working at:

 

1) A deep technical level with engineers, 

2) Describing business functions with analysts,

3) Designing deployments with solution and technical architects,  

4) And pitching On Tracks value to senior executives. 

 

Individual responsibilities include assisting our strategic customers with all aspects of a successful On Track deployment ranging from installation, configuration and management of the Oracle Database, WebLogic Server and the On Track application plus development of Java and JavaScript integrations through to use case guidance, business positioning, training, rollout and value measurement.

 

The candidate will be able to contribute to the business development for On Track within the Enterprise Social Collaboration market place.  They will understand the competitive landscape and be able to provide written thought leadership and product direction supporting the expansion and enhancement of the opportunity pipeline.
