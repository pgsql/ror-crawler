Job Title Sales Consulting Snr Director
Location US-Bala Cynwyd- Bala Plaza West
Organization Name IGBU - Oracle Health Insurance
Department Description
The Oracle Health Insurance (OHI) business, based in the Netherlands, has been historically run out of EMEA Consulting which began in 1994 by a customer funded development project. In 2010, the business moved into the Insurance Global Business Unit (IGBU) and the resellable assets have turned into corporate tier 1 products.OHI's applications fill a key gap in the Insurance GBU's footprint as well as provide entry and reference in the US core health insurance market.
Brief Description
Provides direction and specialist knowledge in applying the technology/application to client business. Facilitation of customer product/application understanding through presentations demonstrations and benchmarks; provision of support throughout the sales cycle.
Detailed Description
As part of the Oracle sales team you will direct and manage pre-sales resources throughout the sales cycle. Provides technical/functional leadership to the sales team in the development and implementation of customer applications and customer products. Participates in account planning and strategy development. May publishes technical information of specific applications and/or technical articles. Works with management to define new products. Serves as the company liaison on specific technical projects with customers.
Job Requirements
Assists in the development of short, medium, and long term plans to achieve strategic objectives. Regularly interacts across functional areas with senior management or executives to ensure unit objectives are met. Ability to influence thinking or gain acceptance of others in sensitive situations important. Knowledge of software industry relating to all aspects of ebusiness solutions. Demonstrated leadership skills. Strong presentation skills. Ability to Travel as needed. BA/BS degree or equivalent, advanced degree highly desirable.
Additional Details

Head of OHI Pre-Sales
Responsible for pre-sales customer development; including support of sales cycles with presentations, demonstrations and proposals. Works with Oracle and OHI sales to develop compelling solutions for OHI customers.  As the acknowledged authority on OHI products in the US market, supports all aspects of Sales process.

US OHI Business Development
Responsible for lead generation, positioning, networking and initiating sales cycles for OHI at US Healthcare Payers, both in the commercial and in the government space. Works with IGBU Business Development and OHI sales to develop customer relationships and advance OHI and Oracle solutions in the industry.

US OHI Product Strategy and US OHI Product Management
Responsible for requirements identification and gathering, pertaining to OHI product compliance with US laws and regulations.  Responsible for ensuring coordination with OHI Global Product Management team to meet US requirements. Responsible for establishment and chair of OHI US customer advisory board.  Represents US at OHI Global customer advisory board.
Advises OHI product strategy/product management and IGBU product strategy on an integrated portfolio of Oracle solutions to address US health insurance customer needs. Provides assessment of OHI capabilities in support of decisions to build, partner or acquire with IGBU and other Oracle teams to offer innovations in the marketplace.

US Go-To-Market
Advises IGBU Sales and OHI leadership  on the plan to develop and implement successful market introductions of OHI products. Also advises on alliances and partners.
