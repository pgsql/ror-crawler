Job Title System Administrator 3-IT
Location AUSTIN - Data Center
Organization Name CIT Storage Team
Department Description
The Commercial IT Storage Team provides operational support for all Commercial customers (On Demand, Federal On Demand & CRM On Demand) on standardized infrastructure housed within a GIT Managed Data Center.

Operational support of standardized storage includes:

  • Incident/problem & escalation management
  • Managing storage management processes and documentation
  • Shared Services Team Point of Contact (OC) for storage projects implementation
  • Participate in Proof Of Concepts, Architecture Design, and Capacity Planning activities
Brief Description
Define, design, and implement network communications and solutions within a fast-paced, leading edge database/applications company.
Detailed Description
Perform performance trend analysis and manage the server/network capacity. Propose client configuration and implement technical solutions to enhance and/or troubleshoot the system. Work with others to define, coordinate vendor purchase needs. Responsible for support documentation as well.
Job Requirements
Job duties are varied and complex utilizing independent judgment. May have project lead role. 5 years of related experience in a medium to large network distributed and computing environment. BS in Computer Science or related field.
Additional Details
  • Manage Storage SR queue ( OHSSA-STOR )
  • Provides storage escalations support (Tier 3) for Request Management and CC Team
  • Participate in storage planning projects/activities/discussions for customers and drive the implementation.
  • Track new developments in technology and ensure the infrastructure is upgraded to current.
  • Scheduling and executing maintenance activities to fix known bugs/issues.
  • Track storage uptime statistics and continuous service improvement
  • Work with the Service Design Team in designing tools that can reduce the cost of operations
  • Inventory management, and provide recommendations on maintenance renewals.
  • Based on demand forecast from customers , provide plan/options to customers on how demand can be met.
  • Ensure vendor SLA compliance
  • Backup and implement a DR plan for important storage configuration files and tools.
  • Wherever possible test the solution/fix in test environment before implementation in customer environment
  • Regular review of operational monitoring tools and add/delete metrics as necessary to increase storage uptime
  • Standardization of storage infrastructure and tools to reduce cost of operations
