Department Description
Brief Description
Responsible for a team that develops, applies and maintains quality standards for company products with adherence to both internal and external standards. Develops and executes software test plans. Analyzes and writes test standards and procedures. Maintains documentation of test results. Analyzes test results and recommends corrective actions.
Detailed Description
As a manager in the technical/process QA division, you will lead a team that designs, installs and evaluates quality control methods and systems. You will also develop standards and procedures to provide quality guidance and methods.
Job Requirements
Directs and ensures the implementation of operational policies through subordinate managers. Interacts internally and externally with executive management, providing negotiation of difficult matters to influence policy. BS or MS degree or equivalent experience relevant to functional area. 8 more years of software engineering or related experience.
Additional Details

This candidate would have experience building a QA organization from scratch to 80-100 employees. This is an organization that develops very technical products and tools for building Software and Frameworks.

Preferred Qualifications:

  1. Outstanding Academic Background
  2. Strong management
  3.  QA background with the latest tools and methods of providing high quality testing and deployment of complex enterprise products.  QTP along with other automated tools are being used currently in combination with “home grown” and manual methods of QA.

  4. Experience within a Product Development Company
