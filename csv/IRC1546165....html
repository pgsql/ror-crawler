Job Title Product Marketing Analyst 5
Location ORACLE PLAZA
Organization Name Sun Tech Product Marketing
Department Description
Oracle Systems Product Marketing is responsible for Product Marketing for Oracle Servers, Storage and Software, including market analysis and strategy, business planning and input to product requirements, solution messaging and value proposition development, and content development for Oracle.com,  global marketing campaigns, and targeted sales plays. Our team provides product marketing support for Oracle Enterprise SPARC Servers, Oracle Superclusters, and Oracle Optimized Solutions (SPARC).
Brief Description
Development and implementation of business plans, marketing strategy, and forecasts for a product/service or vertical market.
Detailed Description
As a product analyst you will participate in every stage of the product life cycle to ensure product meets the needs of users. Drive the implementation of programs in support of the marketing strategy, business plans, and forecasts for assigned product lines. Maintains current status of customer specifications for existing and future products. Identify, evaluate, and recommend marketing opportunities in support of product line objectives. Drive product functionality delivering high quality product documentation. Lead products through scheduled release assisting others to manage commitments and resources. Measure and report progress and review deliverables.
Job Requirements
Acknowledged authority within the Corporation. Provides leadership and expertise in the development of new products/services/processes, frequently operating at the leading edge of technology. Demonstrated product/project management experience. Ability to work with Executives. Strong interpersonal skills. Excellent written and verbal communication. Demonstrated product/project management experience. Creative, energetic, and enthusiastic. Team player. Experience using or implementing Oracle, SAP, or Peoplesoft applications. Basic HTML and web technology skills a plus. BA/BS/MBA degree.
Additional Details
This position will focus on market analysis, business planning, and marketing plans to drive awareness, lead generation, and sales pipeline for Oracle SPARC Enterprise server based solutions, working closely with product management and product development teams to develop integrated systems messaging, value props, proof points and evidence for use in campaign and GTM materials that will be executed through corporate campaigns marketing and field marketing. Also will be responsible for identifying key market opportunities and sales plays, and creating supporting sales tools and content to accelerate SPARC server sales and market share. Need to have experience with Enterprise server market and business analysis, product marketing product management, go to market and demand generation content development.  Experience with Enterprise software and solutions marketing and sales tools development a plus.
