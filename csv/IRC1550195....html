Department Description

Global License Management Services (LMS) is an organization that promotes the management, governance and awareness of the proper use and distribution for Oracle systems through expert services.  LMS’ goals are to Educate, Equip and Enable our customers.  This is achieved by providing the most up-to-date knowledge, employing best practices and tools to manage and maximize an Oracle license investment.  LMS offers a range of value-added services to address customers’ license management needs that are transparent, consultative and based on proven processes and expert licensing capability. 

LMS engages in remote and on-site reviews, leverage virtual teams through project management principles and collaboratively navigates the reviews.  This is achieved by best matching which service offering suits the customer engagement needs, obtaining an understanding of a customer’s IT environment and then recommending proper deployment of licenses. 

 

Through these activities, we aim to transfer knowledge to our customers and partners throughout the engagement process to:

·         Educate on Oracle licensing & business practices and their application

·         Equip with the skills, knowledge and/or tools needed to effectively manage Oracle licenses

·         Enable pro-active license control & management and the realization of the associated benefits

All services performed call for having a handle on a customer’s Oracle IT environment, including Oracle Applications, third party (or custom), and underlying system architecture.  Some service types also entail the execution of system queries and installation of custom measurement scripts to obtain product usage data.

Brief Description
Manages the audits of customers to ensure their license usage complies with their existing contracts. Responsible for people management.
Detailed Description
Develops and manages the audit process of customer licensing, resulting in the generation of revenue and customer satisfaction targets, in cooperation with Sales. Assesses and determines strategic and business development objectives in assigned regions. Responsible for meeting/exceeding revenue and customer satisfaction target. Manages licensing analysts and/or managers and assists on projects where appropriate. Develops review strategy and methodology. Negotiates system review results with customers. Reconciles software usage against license agreements and presents findings to customer IT and senior management.

Leads a specialized area which may have diverse functional elements. Frequently interacts with supervisors and/or functional peer group managers. May interact with senior management. Strong written and verbal communication skills. Strong analytical/business skills. Technical knowledge of Oracle products, UNIX operating systems, network architecture a plus. 5 years experience in IT consulting or audit; including 2 years management experience. Strong presentation skills. Ability to deal with customers in complex business situations. Demonstrated leaderships skills. BA/BS/MBA degree.
Additional Details

The LMS Manager is a key leadership position in LMS that provides guidance and training to its direct reports and helps set and drive the strategic direction of Oracle LMS.

 

Core Duties:

§  Promote LMS regional team initiatives in coordination with LMS NAMER and LMS Global mission, vision and values.

§  Recruit, talent manage and drive retention of direct reports.  Foster team development by setting developmental milestones and goals.  Manage milestones and progress of direct reports and prioritize the team’s support prerequisite.

§  Actively build internal and external relationships by establishing trust through knowledge sharing, licensing guidance, and transparent engagement activities. Market LMS service offerings and build business relationships between Oracle LMS, Oracle Customers and internal Oracle LOBs.

§  Develop and execute a territory plan to maximize certification coverage of existing and newly executed license agreements.  

§  Generate and achieve monthly territory forecasts, and accurately predicts and report revenue on a monthly basis.  Develop and monitor metrics to measure other key performance indicators and group performance. Conducts monthly/quarterly progress meetings with sales team.

§  Support team to qualify large volume of engagements to determine the scope and approach through data mining, interpretation of license agreements, analysis of transaction history and understanding the customer relationship with Oracle.  

§  Monitor execution of established review processes closely in order to evaluate whether changes are necessary given business climate, customer population, and contract terms.  Collaborate with Global Process Owner to drive implementation of necessary changes.

§  Guide the team in the interpretation of customer agreements and support records, and the comparison of these to customers’ usage of Oracle.  Ensure adherence to Oracle’s licensing terms and compliance policy.

§  Work with direct customers to ensure compliance and education of Oracle’s licensing policies. 

§  Manage expectations of constituents involved in LMS engagement by working to resolve complex licensing issues leveraging Oracle’s contracts and business policies and presenting different options to achieve resolution.  Work with direct reports to develop creative approaches to resolve license reviews. 

§  Work in tandem with Business Practices groups to resolve complex licensing issues.  Provide feedback to Business Practices on customer interpretations of Oracle's licensing policies.

§  Promote and support quality assurance throughout the review process and in delivery of compliance reports.

§  Develop and build the core audit program by expanding our ability to audit software usage. 

§  Handle adversarial discussions professionally, influentially, and politically.  Manage customer and sales escalations to support the team with license review resolution.

§  Maintain current and historical knowledge of LMS audit tools, processes, policies and technical prerequisites and advise direct reports on the best approach to measure usage. Pursue continued understanding of corporate, organizational and licensing updates as it relates to LMS activities.
