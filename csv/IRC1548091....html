Department Description

Oracle’s Service Engineering organization provides primary engineering support to Oracle’s Cloud Services and Advanced Customer Services businesses.  Engineering solutions are a core foundation for differentiating these businesses in the marketplace.  Solution scope includes end user and backend delivery applications, automation frameworks, systems management and deployment architectures. 
Brief Description
Manage a team that designs, develops, troubleshoots and debugs software programs for databases, applications, tools, networks etc.
Detailed Description
As a Senior Director of the software engineering division, you will apply your extensive knowledge of software architecture to manage software development tasks associated with developing, debugging or designing software applications, operating systems and databases according to current and future design specifications. Build enhancements within an existing software architecture and envision improvements to the architecture.
Job Requirements
Directs and ensures the implementation of operational policies through subordinate managers Interacts internally and externally with executive management involving negotiation of difficult matters to influence policy. Functional expertise and broad company knowledge. Demonstrated leadership and people management skills. Strong communication skills, analytical skills, thorough understanding of product development. BS or MS degree or equivalent experience relevant to functional area. 8 or more years of software engineering or related experience.
Additional Details

Position Overview

Working with Oracle’s senior management team, business stakeholders, IT operations, and technology teams, set technology direction, positioning, and new processes required to meet Cloud Services go-to-market and delivery goals for managing Fusion Applications.  A critical responsibility of this role is to contribute to the planning, coordination, execution, and integration across the different teams implementing the Fusion Application delivery platform in Cloud Services.    

 

The position will report to the Senior Vice President, Service Engineering. 

 

Preferred Location:  Redwood Shores, California.      

Specific Responsibilities

·         Work with the Service Engineering implementation teams and product management to define engineering and architectural requirements, roadmaps, and opportunities for offering Fusion Application Services to customers. 

 

·         Develop technology requirements with Service Product Management for engineering implementation teams.  Must be able to work with business stakeholders to translate technology opportunity into competitive advantage for service offerings.   

 

·         Work with IT and software management delivery teams to understand and identify opportunities for applying engineering to help reduce costs and improve service levels for managing Fusion Applications.  This role will have a deep understanding of existing operational processes and propose new process approaches utilizing engineering to obtain maximum advantage. 

 

·         Drive technical requirements into Oracle’s Fusion development team on behalf of Service Engineering.  It is critical that this role is able to identify opportunities to make Oracle’s Fusion Application products easier to run, operate, and maintain, and help Oracle translate that into a competitive differentiator for our products and services.

 

·         Develop core technology materials that can be translated into market positioning.  Includes technical white papers and presentations to describe unique technology and delivery capabilities within Cloud Services.

 

·         Communicate Fusion Application Services technology strategy to management, staff, customers, partners, and stakeholders.  Present the technology value and differentiation relative to the overall Cloud Services value proposition.

 

·         Ensure that technology standards and best practices are maintained as Fusion Application Services are implemented across Service Engineering teams.  Play a critical role in project engineering reviews to identify design, security, usability, and quality issues.  Recommend alternatives and investment options.
