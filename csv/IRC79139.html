Job Title Development Systems New Product Project Manager
Location San Jose, CA, US
Organization Name Development Systems Operations
Detailed Description

Project manager for Development Systems new product releases and product lifecycle activities:

  • Work collaboratively with a broad range of partners across geographies and management levels to ensure on time project completion and visibility to project status
  • Create and continuously improve project management methodology and tools

  • Establish new product introduction plans of record; track and coordinate schedule changes with cross functional stakeholders and operations teams

  • Coordinate new product planning, revision, and obsolescence activities with Singapore based supply chain team

  • Partner with cross functional stakeholders to optimize the cost, quality, compliance and market impact of Development Systems products
  • Lead the product discontinuation and product change notice process for DevSys products
  • In partnership with compliance and legal teams, establish policies and requirements necessary to comply with global product safety, environmental, and import compliance requirements
  • Create and maintain documentation for key product release management policies and processes
  • Develop and report key performance indicators

Job Requirements
  • Bachelor’s degree with a minimum of 5+ years experience with software and hardware industry.  Semiconductor industry experience a plus.

  • Experience managing new product introduction projects and processes. PMP certification a plus.
  • Experience with managing multiple projects across geographies and interaction with all management levels

  • Proven history of achieving business goals in the areas of process improvement, on-time completion, risk mitigation, and schedule recovery

  • Knowledge of supply chain operations and document control processes, strongly desired

  • Experience with Matrix software, MS Project, MS Excel, Oracle ERP systems desired

  • Independent thinker and self motivated driver of projects

  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills
