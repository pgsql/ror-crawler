Department Description

The Applications Development IT team is responsible for implementing, running, and improving nearly all of Oracle’s Enterprise Applications: CRM, HCM, Financials, SCM, you name it. We ensure Oracle is always Oracle’s best reference for our enterprise applications. In this role, we often race out ahead of standard product and build extensions and custom applications to meet the needs of our Fortune 100 company. Once proven, our ideas and innovations quickly find their way back into core product. ADIT’s SaaS team is leveraging the full power of the Oracle stack – including Sun hardware, the world’s best-selling database and middleware, and enterprise strength applications – into compelling cloud solutions that are easy to try, easy to use, and easy to buy. 
Brief Description
Work with a world class team to develop, implement, and support cutting edge Oracle technology.
Detailed Description
Manages a team supporting and/or implementing software project(s) and/or internal systems. Defines, documents and manages scope, expectations, implementation approach, deliverables and acceptance testing criteria.
Job Requirements
Manages and controls activities in multi-functional areas or sections. Ensures appropriate operational planning is effectively executed to meet Corporate specifications. Successful track record in applications implementation and as a manager. Expert knowledge of all applications within a business area; plus some knowledge of other applications. BA/BS degree preferred.
Additional Details

•     The SaaS Customer Experience Manager will be responsible for rapidly hiring and managing a group that includes:

1. A team of up to 10 click-to-chat and click-to-dial support representatives in two countries. The manager will be responsible for hiring and training these reps to provide help at the point where users need it most – right from within the software.

In SaaS (Software as a Service) offering, live trial is critical to the sales process, and helping new users get comfortable and productive is critical to retention. The difference between success and failure is the user’s ability to quickly become comfortable with the software and use it to perform the critical processes of their job. The SaaS Customer Support team will provide support via click-to-chat and click-to-dial to make sure that users have the best possible experience with Oracle’s SaaS offerings, transition smoothly from trial to purchase, and continue to have a positive experience in daily use on the job.

 

2. A demo data and tutorial development team of up to 3 people responsible for creating data and tutorials that help trial users quickly learn and become productive with Oracle’s SaaS software.
