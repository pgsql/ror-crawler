Department Description
Oracle Fusion Price Management enables companies to quickly define and implement sophisticated pricing strategies to meet rapidly changing business needs. Fusion price Management provides full picture of the price management space which includes prices, adjustments, discounts, costs and margins. Using fusion Price Management customers can track and identify any revenue leakages happening within the enterprise. The product will be developed based upon state of the art fusion middleware based pricing engine, ADF based administration capabilities, that can be tailored by customers to meet their business needs. Fusion Price Management has many consumers in fusion applications and outside of Fusion such as Quote and Order Capture, Service Contracts, Sales Contracts, Inventory, Costing etc.. Fusion Price Management also integrates with fusion products like Inventory Costing, Tax, Shipping etc.. to obtain the various pricing and costing elements needed for effective execution price management. Fusion pricing will provide analytical capabilities for analysing and monitoring the effectiveness of Pricing Strategies for a given Enterprise.
