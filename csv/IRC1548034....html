Department Description

Oracle Business Intelligence Applications provide prepackaged analytical dashboard and reporting functionality across all common ERP sources such as Oracle E-Business Suite, SAP, PeopleSoft, Siebel, JDEdwards, etc. Oracle Business Intelligence Applications are hot-pluggable to the largest number of sources systems and database platforms, and are the most comprehensive BI applications in the market.

 

Simply put, customers use Oracle BI Applications to rapidly deploy pre-defined dashboards against their ERP data sources. These dashboards provide a wide range of insight in areas such as finance, sales, marketing, human resources, supply chain, procurement etc. Leveraging these dashboards, employees of any company can make decisions that grow revenue, cut unwanted spend and significantly boost the bottom line of the company.

 

Oracle BI Applications are built on a sophisticated technology stack. They leverage a state of the art data integration platform and the industry’s most advanced analytical business intelligence platform. They are also tightly integrated with Oracle’s market leading Fusion Middleware platform and Oracle’s market leading database.

 

The result – Oracle BI Applications are one of the fastest growing areas of Oracle’s software product portfolio. The largest retailer in the world uses Oracle BI applications. The largest telecommunications company in the world relies on Oracle BI applications.  We build a product set that every business in the world can benefit from.
