Department Description

Systems Management division of Oracle delivers two flagship products Oracle Enterprise Manager and Oracle Configuration Manager.

Oracle Enterprise Manager offers

·        Complete, Open, Integrated Management of Oracle Technologies.

·        Deep, Optimized, Best of Breed Solutions for Database, Middleware, Packaged Applications, Physical and Vritual Infrastructure

·        Business Centric, Top Down Application Management

·        Complete Lifecycle Management

·        Scalable Grid Management

 

Oracle Configuration Manager (OCM) along with Software Configuration Manager (SCM) enables Oracle to deliver:

 

·        Faster time to problem resolution

·        Service Request (SR) management and tracking for your configurations

·        Security patch advice and health checks specific to your configurations

·        Knowledge content tailored for your configurations

 

This is a strategic area of expansion for Oracle as we provide a more integrated easy-to-use Oracle suite of products in a networked world. Be a part of this dynamic, energetic team that is responsible for this new strategic direction.
