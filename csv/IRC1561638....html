Department Description
Fusion Customer Data Management (CDM) is designed to manage enterprise wide trading community information. It provides unified schema & services (Trading Community Model) and set of tools (Trading Community Data Quality, Trading Community Hub & Trading Community Policies) to consolidate, cleanse and enrich trading community information. All Fusion applications will use these products for storing and accessing trading community information centrally e.g. information about customers, suppliers, individual consumers, company contacts. These set of products are better known in the industry and inside Oracle as Trading Community Architecture or TCA.
