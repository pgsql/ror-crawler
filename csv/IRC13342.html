Job Summary

About this Position: This position will support the sales hiring objectives.

 The purpose of the Sales Recruiter position is to:

 

  • Identify sourcing techniques to attract the highest quality of Sales candidates and build a consistent candidate pool

  • Pre-screen resumes, screen candidates, and assist in Sales hiring decision

  • Implement a process for tracking, prioritizing, and reporting on the status of staffing activities

  • Support HR projects as needed

     

Position KSAs: What Knowledge, Skills and Abilities will you need to succeed in this role?

 

  • You need to have outstanding written and verbal communication skills.

  • You need to possess initiative and manage multiple priorities well.

  • You need to be self-directed, assertive, and convey an approachable style.

  • You need to be Process driven.
Job Details

Position Outcomes & Activities: You will be measured on your ability to perform the following activities effectively.

 

  • Quality of candidates you produce

  • Creatively source candidates for sales positions throughout the U.S.

  • Provide detailed feedback on each candidates strengths and weaknesses

  • Build and maintain a candidate network for future openings

  • Build strong relationships with hiring managers to ensure hiring requirements are met

  • Ensure positive applicant experience throughout the hiring process

 

Additional Position Requirements: Experience and Education requirements.

 

  • Bachelor’s degree (preferably in the field of HR, Business)

  • At least 5 years of solid staffing experience

  • Previous experience with behavioral based interviewing, pre-employment tests, and other innovative selection methods. 

  • Experience with high volume recruitment and selection of salaried positions, ideally in a sales environment.

  • Proficient in Microsoft Word, Excel, Outlook, PowerPoint, SharePoint
  • Knowledge of federal and state employment law
