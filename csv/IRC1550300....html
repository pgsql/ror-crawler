Brief Description

Detailed Description
Conducts business reviews of Oracle partners to determine if the Oracle partner is in compliance with the terms and conditions of its Oracle agreement(s) and Oracle's pricing, licensing, and business practices. Works with partner to resolve compliance issues and ensures proper compensation to Oracle while preserving the partner relationship. Solicits and evaluates potential targets for review and travels to their places of business to perform the business reviews. Interprets and summarizes partner agreements, reviews, and reconciles software sales activity and accompanies support records, and work with other Oracle organizations such as Royalty Reporting, Order Administration, Contracts, Alliances, Support, Business Practices, Legal, and Sales.

Overview

The primary function of the Licence Management Service (LMS) is to work with customers and partners to ensure contractual compliance and to provide feedback on licensing in the marketplace.  The team in ANZ is part of a successful operation throughout Asia Pacific tasked with conducting a variety of reviews with customers and partners, achieving customer satisfaction through the provision of a professional service and maximising revenue owed to Oracle for contractual compliance.

 

The Senior LMS Consultant is chartered with performing impartial and co-operative reviews with customers and partners to:

  • establish whether they are operating within the terms of their agreements with Oracle;

  • determine whether they are getting the best use out of their investment in Oracle products and services; and

  • ensure they are made aware of any additional Oracle products or services that may be of value to their organisation.

 

This role is a great opportunity to join a highly successful technology based business.  It offers variety, customer contact, autonomy and the chance to have a real impact.

 

It is anticipated that the responsibilities of the person going into this role will increase over time, as will their seniority within our business.

 

Responsibilities

  • Develop and constantly maintain a very high level understanding of Oracle products and their associated licensing terms and conditions  as well as Oracle’s business practices.
  • Work as part of the LMS ANZ team and achieve goals on a quarterly and yearly basis.

  • Plan and execute LMS engagements such as Reviews, and Campaigns.
  • Advice on suitability of licensing structures in meeting and optimizing the licensing of Customer's or Partner's business and system environments.

  • Work with Oracle customers and partners to understand their utilisation of Oracle products and services.
  • Present compliance findings/reports to customers and partners.
  • Provide customers and partners with an assessment of current and future licensing needs  and Education of Oracle licensing policies and business practices.
  • Build strong working relationships with  other internal departments such as Sales, Channels, Finance, Legal & Support in country.  

  • Domestic travel (approx 30% of the time) within ANZ is anticipated.
