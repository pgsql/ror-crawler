Job Title Engagement Manager

Department Description

We are seeking an  Engagement Manager in Santa Clara. This person will be responsible for Engagement Management for Java Licensees and customers in America starting from pre-sales engagement through post-sales business support and ongoing relationship management.  The Engagement Manager (EM)  will be responsible for managing all Java Licensees in North and Latin Americas. The roles of the Engagement Manager consist of:

  • Business Management: for a set of accounts, the EM is responsible for supporting the sales pipeline.
  • Account Management: Manage client relationship and provide customer satisfaction.
  • Monitoring the entire lifecycle of an engagement from initial scoping and contracting through close of the engagement with the customer.

  • Reporting the progress, ensure readiness and recommend enhancements.

  • Partnering: as a member of a virtual account team, the EM manages the expectations of a wide range of people which includes the Sales team, the Customer Engineering team and the Product Management.

 

Department Description:

 

The Java Mobile Embedded organization  helps OEMs deploy Java platforms on smart cards, mobile phones, embedded devices, media solutions, personal computers and servers, by either licensing source code to these platforms or by providing complete binary solutions.  Java Mobile Embedded supports its licensees in all the phases of the product cycle: development, deployment, customization, support, compliance and certification. The Engagement Team has a worldwide presence and provides account  management and services delivery by continuously  engaging with customers.

 

Job Responsibilities:

 

  • Supporting overall account management, growth, and leadership of cross-functional teams to develop approaches and targets that meet the business goals of our clients.

  • Responsible for developing engagement strategy tracks within a client relationship, and working with engineering leads and project teams to execute on plans, involving people at optimal levels to maximize utilization targets.

  • Maintain a strong working relationship with the client, including a post-engagement relationship to possibly introduce new opportunities.
  • Manage profitability of account tracks by developing and negotiating statements of work, ensuring work is delivered on budget.

  • Consistently identify needs client may not recognize and ensure that approaches and solutions are linked to current business objectives and future needs.

  • Consistently manage client expectations, ensure delivery of the highest quality service, and solicit and act on client feedback.

  • Resolve team conflicts and make strategic and tactical decisions to drive the engagement towards completion.

  • Serve as a point of escalation; actively minimize any risk associated with a project before it reaches the client.
  • Strong presentation, verbal and written skills as well as proven ability to develop well-written documents and presentations.

  • Participate in strategic development of new business.
