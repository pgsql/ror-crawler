Sr. Director, Oracle University US Sales

 

Job Description

 

Oracle University offers the most comprehensive selection of quality training available today for Oracle internet based technologies and Oracle's Applications.  OU is the world's leading Applications education provider for IT professionals!   We offer more than 120 instructor-led training (ILT) courses in state-of-the-art education centers in 63 countries around the world.  We offer organizations a diverse training program unparalleled in flexibility!

 

The candidate we seek is an innovative, dynamic, high-energy, sales executive that brings a strong record of performance in a telesales environment.   The primary responsibility is in managing and developing the overall sales effort for Oracle University.  The team includes over 100 professionals selling Instructor Led Training and Education products primarily through two Sales Centers located in Reston, Virgina and Rocklin, California, complimented by a sales consulting staff in the field as well as demand generation in Bangalore, India.  This role reports directly to the Vice President of Oracle University (OU) and is responsible for generating over $200M in sales.  The ability to work in a fast paced, team oriented, constantly changing environment is crucial to this role.    Approximately 50% travel required depending on the location of the candidate.

 

Responsibilities:

 

-          Set and maintain an overall vision and provide leadership for the OU US Sales organization

-          Daily management of all OU US Sales Centers

-          Manage multiple cost centers including weekly forecasting and budgeting

-          Directly responsible for $200M quota

-          Valued contributor of the global OU Management Team

-          Furnishes innovated ideas to motivate sales staff and drive sales

 

Qualifications:

 

·         Minimum of ten to twelve (10-12) years related professional experience and five to seven (5-7) years of management experience

·         B.S. required, M.B.A a plus

·         Extensive, proven success as a senior sales leader

·         Proven experience in managing a multi-faceted, complex, distributed sales organization in a high pressure environment

·         Proven leadership and organizational skills

·         Proven sales success - on or above quota attainment for the last 3 years

·         Strong working knowledge of Sales Center management and operations

·         Very focused on delivering results and maintaining a high degree of integrity
Brief Description
Order fulfillment and selling of products and services in high volume, low value, short cycle transactions using telephone sales.
Detailed Description
Manages a team of telesales managers for a territory/region. Participates as a team member in strategic and tactical planning for the division. Develops working relationships with license, consulting, education, and/or support field managers to develop joint account plans. Develops and executes strategic regional plans utilizing resources from various marketing and sales functions to maximize revenue. Responsible for coaching, performance management, and career development of staff. Participates in the recruiting process and provides hiring recommendations to senior management. Handles escalations and participates directly in complex sales situations to negotiate and/or resolve disputes and ensure customer satisfaction. Conducts regional sales forecasts, territory planning, demand generation, deal strategy development and trend analysis.
Job Requirements
Directs and ensures the implementation of operational policies through subordinate managers. Interacts internally and externally with executive management involving negotiation of difficult matters to influence policy. Functional expertise and broad company knowledge.
