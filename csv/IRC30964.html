Job Title Account Services Associate
Location HR - Mechanicsburg, PA
Organization Name Sales - Account Management
Department Description
Brief Description

The incumbent in this position provides project management support to the Sales Department and oversees the implementation of benefit changes and new divisions to existing business in their territory.
Detailed Description
  • Manages the implementation process for benefit changes and new divisions for existing business.

  • Implements New Business when needed.

  • Acts as liaison between external contact and internal departments.

  • Provides a point of contact to clients regarding implementation; attend open enrollment meetings and benefit fairs as necessary; coordinate training for web-based systems.

  • Assists with development and delivery of implementation communication materials.
  • Performs miscellaneous duties as assigned.
Job Requirements
  • BA/BS degree, preferably in Business Administration or Marketing, or Equivalent Experience

  • 3 to 5 years Delta Dental experience

  • Experience in customer communications, client service

  • Experience in developing/maintaining customer relationships

  • Insurance license will be required

  • Detail orientation and problem-solving capabilities

  • Customer service focus and strong customer service skills

  • Strong organizational/time management and project management skills and multi-tasking abilities; ability to change priorities as needed
  • Ability to meet time-sensitive external market driven deadlines
  • Ability to work with others in a team environment
  • Ability to communicate effectively and professionally with brokers, consultants, and clients

  • Proven organization and follow-up skills

  • Familiarity with service guarantees, SPDs/EOCs, and contractual language preferred
  • Knowledge of Delta Dental products, policies, claims, eligibility, billing, and underwriting guidelines

  • Selling and negotiation skills
  • Knowledge of Current Dental Terminology ("CDT") codes and dental nomenclature

  • Familiarity with DDP and competitor products and services available

  • Public speaking skills

  • Excellent oral and written communication skills

  • PC literacy required; MSOffice skills (Outlook, Word, Excel, PowerPoint)
  • Regularly required to work standard office equipment (personal computer, photocopy machine, fax machine, etc.)

  • Ability to work on a computer up to 7 hours a day

  • Regularly required to sit for long periods of time, and occasionally stand and walk

  • Regularly required to use hands to operate computer and other office equipment

  • Close vision required for computer usage

  • Occasionally required to stoop, kneel, climb and lift up to 25 pounds

  • Must be able to understand fairly complex written and oral instructions

  • Ability to clearly present written information and findings

  • Ability to communicate concepts - Presentation skills required for open enrollment events and client meetings

  • Ability to interact well with co-workers and outside contacts
