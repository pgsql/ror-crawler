Department Description
The Oracle Retail Global Business Unit (RGBU) develops and supports a complete suite of enterprise applications for top tier retailers around the world. See details at Oracle's Retail site
The MOM (Merchandising Operations and Management) Quality Assurance (QA) team is responsible for ensuring high quality assurance and control across the Merchandising suite of applications and middleware.
Brief Description
Responsible for developing, applying and maintaining quality standards for company products with adherence to both internal and external standards. Develops and executes software test plans. Analyzes and writes test standards and procedures. Maintains documentation of test results. Analyzes test results and recommends corrective actions.
Detailed Description
As a member of the technical/process QA division, you will design, install and evaluate quality control methods and systems. Design continuing maintenance of product quality. Develop standards and procedures to provide quality guidance and methods.

Additional Details

Job Description
As the QC tester for the Oracle RGBU you will be responsible for the day to day test and supporting deliverables for your application(s). You will develop and execute test cases for Java or Forms based Oracle Retail applications and send status reports to your test lead. You will also work with the test lead on creating master test plans and in developing and following standard quality procedures within your project. You will also be responsible for reviewing requirements and design documents, operations and user guide, writing test cases and ensuring adequate test coverage for the same. The job requires familiarity with different test methodologies and the ability to apply them in the day-to-day work.

Ideal candidate possesses a strong technical background and familiarity with QA concepts and methodology, excellent verbal and written skills to work collaboratively with various development teams. Primary responsibility would be to work with the QA leads on the following:
1) Installing and deploying Java based applications based on installation guide instructions
2) Developing and executing test cases for Java and Forms based applications
3) Working on test plans and test case authoring
4) Compiling reports and updates on general project status

As a member of the technical/process QA division, you will design, install and evaluate quality control methods and systems. Design continuing maintenance of product quality. Develop standards and procedures to provide quality guidance and methods.
