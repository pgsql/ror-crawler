Department Description

Advanced Customer Services (ACS) provides expert, customer focused advice and assistance to supplement and improve IT management. The services will:

  • Reduce critical issues and improve availability

  • Optimize performance of Oracle environments

  • Improve system continuity

  • Standardize change and minimize its impact

  • Improve alignment with your business needs

  • Partner to provide proactive guidance

  • Plan for future needs

 

The ACS SST provides engineering and management resources to Oracle customers throughout the United States and Canada which consists of:

·         Core DBAs server, application server, tools, etc.

·         Apps DBAs including PeopleSoft, Oblix, JDE, Retek, Siebel technical stack

·         Mid tier including BEA, Hyperion, Stellent and legacy Oracle mid tier technology.

·        Service Delivery Management customer and program management.


SST SDEs (Service Delivery Engineers) are senior DBAs well versed in multiple Oracle components and have excellent triage and problem diagnostic skills.  SDEs obtain appropriate certifications and maintain expert level competency on new features and products. 

 

SDEs skill sets include but are not limited to: 

  • installations

  • upgrades

  • migration

  • performance tuning

  • trouble shooting

  • test case reproduction

  • capacity planning

  • root cause analysis

  • patching

  • infrastructure review

  • other scheduled services

 

SST SDMs (Service Delivery Managers) provide support management services.  Most, if not all, are ITIL and PMP certified and manage the following:

  • Escalations

  • Situations

  • Issues

  • Resources

  • Projects

  • Inter-LOB including Development, Education and Consulting

 

Additionally, competencies of SST members also include:

  • Strong communication skills both verbal & written

  • Professional

  • Proactive

  • Team player

  • Handles multiple tasks efficiently

 

The SST delivers scheduled services installations, migrations, as well as, assisted services to name a few.  Assignments are typically short term assignments.   SST team members reside anywhere in the United States or Canada.  Travel is 75%.

 

The SST provides a professional with the opportunity for hands-on experience within various Oracle products, multiple platforms/footprints and a variety of industries.

Brief Description
As a member of the Support organization, your focus is to deliver post-sales support and solutions to the Oracle customer base while serving as an advocate for customer needs. This involves resolving post-sales non-technical customer inquiries via phone and electronic means, as well as, technical questions regarding the use of and troubleshooting for our Electronic Support Services. A primary point of contact for customers, you are responsible for facilitating customer relationships with Support and providing advice and assistance to internal Oracle employees on diverse customer situations and escalated issues.
Detailed Description
As an Advisory Systems Engineer, you are expected to be an expert member of the problem-solving/avoidance team and be highly skilled in solving extremely complex (often previously unknown), critical customer issues. Performing the assigned duties with a high level of autonomy and reporting to management on customer status and technical matters on a regular basis, you will be expected to work with very limited guidance from management. Further, the Advisory Systems Engineer is sought by customers and Oracle employees to provide expert technical advice.
Job Requirements
Leading contributor individually and as a team member, providing direction and mentoring to others. Work is non-routine and very complex, involving the application of advanced technical/business skills in area of specialization. Because of substantial customer interfacing, a demonstrated ability to work with customers on an independent basis with exceptional communication skills, while consistently achieving the absolute highest levels of customer satisfaction. Both a Bachelor*s and Master*s degree in Computer Science, Engineering or equivalent experience 8 years related experience prior to taking this position. In addition, experience with Oracle*s core products, applications, and tools.
Additional Details

We are seeking an APPS DBA / RAT tester with knowledge of Real application testing suite.

Responsibilities.

Create, maintain and control access to databases required to support the implementation of Oracle Applications.

Assist with capacity planning.

Perform database sizing and prepare other database environments accordingly (e.g. development, training, conversion, system test, production etc.)

Assist development team with sizing for data conversion and interfaces.

Perform database monitoring for space utilization and take pro-active measures.

Perform database monitoring for performance and take appropriate actions, including recommendations of changes to INIT.ORA to UNIX Administrator.

Review any database changes which are required as either part of an Oracle ‘patch’ or as part of a change request from the development team.

Quality Assuring any SQL code produced by the development team to ensure that it will perform satisfactorily.

Regularly validate the database backup and recovery strategy

Create and maintain the Applications environments, in conjunction with the UNIX Administrator.

Assist the Project team with defining the batch processing and scheduling requirements and implementation.

Define, configure and maintain Concurrent Managers, Workshifts and  specialization rules to meet both the on-line requirements and the batch processing requirements.

Maintain details of customized concurrent executables, programs and parameters as defined by the development team.

Define, configure and maintain printers, printer styles and printer drivers.

Define and maintain, in conjunction with the Oracle DBA, the Oracle Ids, Datagroups and audit trails

Define and maintain some of the Applications system profile - where they pertain to Operational aspects (e.g. setting of ‘trace’ for concurrent programs)

Define and maintain custom Applications as required by the Development team and in conjunction with the UNIX Administrator.

Assist Project team with investigation and problem resolution where it pertains to the ‘standard’ Applications software/database.

This individual requires to work on Real application testing suite. You will be required to create testing scripts, execute them and tracking defects.
