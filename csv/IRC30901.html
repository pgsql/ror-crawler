Brief Description

Entry level marketing position available for a detail oriented, self-starter who loves a challenging work environment.  Local insurance company is looking for a candidate with exceptional writing and communications skills. Candidate must demonstrate above average verbal and written communications, strong computer skills, and basic familiarity with social media channels.

Detailed Description

Time will be divided between assisting with integrated marketing projects and collateral fulfillment.

 

Direct Marketing

  • Assisting in creating and implementing direct marketing campaigns through print, electronic and social media channels

  • Contributing content for segment and product collateral

  • Shepherding projects through channels including business stakeholders, compliance and creative services

  • Making ongoing collateral revisions/updates for account sales and service and for clients

Print Fulfillment

  • Producing marketing collateral and mailings in a deadline-driven environment

  • Placing print orders with outside vendors; entering and tracking purchase orders for drop ship and stock items

  • Performing administrative support such as quality check on fulfillment materials and stock
Job Requirements
  • College Degree or equivalent. Background in journalism or marketing a strong plus

  • Must be detail oriented, able to multi-task and work independently

  • Math, proofreading, spelling, and computer proficiency including Word, Excel, Access. (Visio and Adobe InDesign or Acrobat Professional a plus)

  • Experience with copiers and/or print fulfillment and office equipment preferred

  • Must be able to work overtime

  • Must be able to lift small boxes for packaging and shipping
