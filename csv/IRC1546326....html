Title: License Consultant

Organization: License Management Services  (LMS)

Location: Redwood Shores (CA), Burlington (MA),

 

Department Description:

 

Global License Management Services (LMS) is an organization that promotes the management, governance and awareness of the proper use and distribution for Oracle systems through expert services.  LMS’ goals are to Educate, Equip and Enable our customers/partners.  This is achieved by providing the most up-to-date knowledge, employing best practices and tools to manage and maximize an Oracle license investment.  LMS offers a range of value-added services to address customers’ license management needs that are transparent and consultative, based on proven processes and expert licensing capability.  Please use the following link to learn more about the LMS Organization and our service offerings: License Management Services.

 

Brief Posting Description:

 

The License Consultant works on LMS engagements remotely, leverages virtual teams through project management principles and collaboratively navigates the engagement from beginning to end.  This is achieved by best matching which service offering suits the customer / partner engagement needs, obtaining an understanding of a customer/ partner’s IT environment and then recommending proper deployment of licenses. 

Through these activities, the Consultant aims to transfer knowledge to our customers and partners throughout the engagement process to:

·         Educate on Oracle licensing & business practices and their application

·         Equip with the skills, knowledge and/or tools required to effectively manage Oracle licenses

·         Enable pro-active license control & management and the realization of the associated benefits

All services performed calls for having an understanding of the customer’s/partner’s Oracle IT environment, including Oracle Applications, third party (or custom), and underlying system architecture.  Some service types also entail the execution of system queries and installation of custom measurement scripts to obtain product usage data.   

 
Brief Description
Performs software license reviews of our partners to ensure compliance with their contract terms and Oracle*s Business Practices while providing value-added services to enhance partner satisfaction.
Detailed Description
Conducts business reviews of Oracle partners to determine if the Oracle partner is in compliance with the terms and conditions of its Oracle agreement(s) and Oracle*s pricing, licensing, and business practices. Works with partner to resolve compliance issues and ensures proper compensation to Oracle while preserving the partner relationship. Solicits and evaluates potential targets for review and travels to their places of business to perform the business reviews. Interprets and summarizes partner agreements, reviews, and reconciles software sales activity and accompanies support records, and work with other Oracle organizations such as Royalty Reporting, Order Administration, Contracts, Alliances, Support, Business Practices, Legal, and Sales. Identify and qualify customer review opportunities. Provide license and business practice advice in support of follow-up sales negotiations. Generate annual license compliance revenue and customer satisfaction targets. Participate in projects to improve the review process, licensing and technical knowledge. Provide licensing and Business Practice education to internal and external customers.
Job Requirements
Duties and tasks are standard with some variation. Completes own role largely independently within defined policies and procedures. Strong interpersonal skills. Ability to comprehend and interpret licensing issues. Project management skills. Strong analytical/business skills. Ability to work in a dynamic fast paced environment. Travel as needed. 2 years experience and BA/BS.

Responsibilities:

§  Self-motivates to independently achieve personal and team LMS goals and objectives.
