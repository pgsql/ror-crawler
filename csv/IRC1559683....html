Department Description

CRM, Customer Relationship Management, is all about taking good care of and providing the best experience for those who are most important to an enterprise - their customers. Critical to the success and step number one of this mission is to enable and facilitate customers getting in touch with customer representatives and have their problems resolved - this is exactly what CRM Contact Center is designed for.

As one of the most important pillars of the CRM suite, the Oracle Fusion Application Contact Center product group is growing and looking for talents to build a world class CRM product. If you want to help Oracle develop from ground up it's Fusion Applications and be part of it's success to take over the number 1 spot as applications vendor, come join our exciting team.

Oracle's Contact Center is an product team in the Applications Division chartered with building and delivering standards-based channel control infrastructure, pre-built integrations and world class UI. The primary objective of the group is to build and deliver SOA components that facilitate the control of media channels between channels providers and applications, as well as advanced routing and management to ensure smooth processing of customer requests. Media channels include but not limited to phone call, VOIP, web conferencing and web chat. Design and development of these components will be leveraging platform infrastructure provided by Fusion Middleware and utilize cutting edge technologies such as J2EE, BPEL and SOA Suite, etc.  On top of infrastructure development, the team also involves in developing a world class User Interface which delivers the best user experiences in using modern day communication channels.

As a member of the software engineering division, you will work with product strategy and applications developers to design and implement core components. You will also leverage knowledge of Java, J2EE, and other design patterns to architect advanced API interfaces that provide an optimal experience for Fusion applications. It's a great time to join the team as we are still at very early phase of Fusion Application development and you have the opportunity to be one of the first members of this team. It's a unique combination being able to stay in the forefront of technology while building a new product with proven market demand. I am sure you'll have a lot of fun, learn a lot with great growth potential. I will be happy to meet with you to discuss further the opportunity.
