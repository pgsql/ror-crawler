Additional Details
Oracle is seeking an SRAM/circuit designer who is interested in the development of next generation chip multi-threaded (CMT) family microprocessor.

This position is for research, design, and implementation of fully custom memory designs, including SRAM, register file, and CAM designs. This position will require the engineer to address challenging design constraints for timing, area, and power, including developing novel and advanced circuit designs to meet objectives.

Essential Functions:
  • Perform circuit design and detailed SPICE simulations of custom transistor-level circuitry for high performance microprocessors in deep-submicron technology
  • Understand micro-architecture, define circuit specifications, capture schematic and perform timing analysis at transistor & static gate level
  • Define circuit design and backend flow methodology with other lead circuit designers
  • Guide junior level designers and work closely with mask designers to supervise layout
  • Establish & manage design schedule

Preferred Knowledge:
  • Extensive knowledge and experiences of front-end design analysis, execution and back-end design verification
  • Expertise in static noise margin, memory cell characterization and self-timed margin analysis
  • Expertise in design for performance (DFM) related to state-of-art technology nodes: 40nm, 28nm and beyond
  • Familiar with SRAM design methodology; prior Sun experiences are excellent pluses
  • Intimate working knowledge of design tools: HSPICE or SPICE-like simulators, schematic/layout entry tools
Related Experience - Senior Level
  • MSEE with 8+ years
  • BSEE with 10+ years
