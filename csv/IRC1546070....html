Department Description
Provide comprehensive 7X24 monitoring and management service on customer environments to improve availability and performance.  Utilize a personalized service management platform integrated with Oracle Premier Support and Development that leverages ITIL methodology, across a global customer base, and wide range of technologies - focused around storage and backup solutions.
Brief Description
As a member of the Support organization, your focus is to deliver post-sales support and solutions to the Oracle customer base while serving as an advocate for customer needs. This involves resolving post-sales non-technical customer inquiries via phone and electronic means, as well as, technical questions regarding the use of and troubleshooting for our Electronic Support Services. A primary point of contact for customers, you are responsible for facilitating customer relationships with Support and providing advice and assistance to internal Oracle employees on diverse customer situations and escalated issues.
Detailed Description
As a Senior Systems Engineer, you will interface with the customer's IT staff on a regular basis. Either at the client's site or from a remote location, you will be responsible for resolution of moderately complex technical problems related to the installation, recommended maintenance and use and repair/workarounds for Oracle products. You should be highly experienced in some Oracle products and several platforms that are being supported. You will be expected to work with only general guidance from management while advising management on progress/status.
Job Requirements
Job duties are varied and complex utilizing independent judgment. May have project lead role. Because of substantial customer interfacing, a demonstrated ability to work with customers on an independent basis with exceptional communication skills, while consistently achieving the highest levels of customer satisfaction is essential. A Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering or equivalent experience is preferred with five years related experience. Experience with Oracle's core products, applications, and tools is important.
Additional Details

Senior Storage and Backup Systems Administrator

 

DESCRIPTION

 

The successful candidate should have 6 to 8 years of experience doing both storage and backup administration.  Must have a strong command of customer interactions and have the ability to drive technical discussions.  Must exhibit a strong sense of ownership and urgency in handling critical incidents.  The Backup and Storage Administrator will provide system, software and hardware support. Backup tasks include establishing and maintaining backup policies, schedules, recovery, archiving, reporting and troubleshooting as required to meet client expectations.  Storage tasks include the allocations, zoning, installing, maintaining, and troubleshooting of storage systems. 

 

This position requires excellent verbal and written communication skills, strong customer service focus, the ability work in a team as well as independently, ability to learn on the fly, ability to follow procedures, and ability to suggest improvements to existing procedures as appropriate.

 

REQUIRED SKILLS:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Information Technology or Computer Science or equivalent work experience

  • Six plus years of backup management experience

  • Netbackup is required; ArcServe is desired.
  • Five plus year of experience with VTL, DSSU and D2D2T technologies and procedures.
  • Experience creating and managing backup policies

  • Familiar with backups relating to Unix, Windows, VMWare, database (e.g. - Oracle RMON).

  • Experience with tape archiving procedures.

  • Patch management for backup systems.

  • Six plus years experience with SAN concepts and best practices, including the different SAN topologies:

  • Experience with Sun branded SAN technology.

  • Experience with HDS, EMC, HP, NetApp and IBM SANs desirable.
  • Patch management for SAN technologies

  • Switch administration – McData (e.g., Intrepid 6XXX and Sphereon 4xxx), Brocade (e.g., Silkworm 5xxx, DCX Director), Qlogic and Cisco.

  • Familiar with logical functions of switches like zoning and port types, as well as the physical attributes of the different switches.

  • Experience with appropriate storage and switch management tools, e.g., HiCommand (HDS), CAM (Sun / LSI), EFCM / DCFM (switches).

  • The understanding of how storage is presented to the environment and reviewing diagnostic output when necessary to work with SA’s is key.

  • Knowledgeable of creating file systems and partitioning, including volume managers and clustering – especially how these apply to storage.  This includes LVM Disksuite and Veritas Suite.

  • Experience with Oracle ASM and Infiniband a plus.

  • Strong communication and organizational skills.

  • Strong troubleshooting skills

  • Ability to communicate effectively in writing and with end users

  • Ability to share knowledge and experience with others.

  • Ability to provide exceptional customer service.

  • Ability to multitask.

 

DESIRED SKILLS:

  • UNIX administrative experience, Solaris 8 through 10 (Linux, HP-UX, AIX a plus) is desirable.

  •  Project Management skills – some
  •  Candidates must be highly motivated, must have initiative,  team-oriented, and able to meet the aggressive schedules required in a fast moving environment

 

CERTIFICATIONS:

ADDITIONAL DELIVERABLES:

  • Tape management and offsite coordination.

  • Providing detailed documentation for reporting, procedural, problem resolution and planning purposes.
