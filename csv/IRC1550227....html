Department Description

Oracle Financing is in search of an experienced and successful leasing and financing sales professional to be added to our Enterprise sales team.  Initially this role will support the North American Enterprise Software sales team through driving additional support and awareness across the Oracle sales force with a goal of helping Oracle close more business.  Only those with proven sales experience to the Fortune 500 in the leasing and financial services industry should apply.  Experience in IT financing and leasing in the Enterprise marketspace is a plus.

 

About the Oracle Financing Division (OFD):

 

Oracle Financing, a subsidiary of Oracle Corporation, is chartered to provide customers with financing and leasing solutions for their acquisition of Oracle’s and other third party’s IT products and services.

 

About the Sales Representative Position:

 

Oracle Financing is in search of a high energy commercial financing sales professional to be added to our sales team. Only those with proven sales experience should apply. Experience in IT financing and leasing is a plus.  This is a non-management role.

 

Preferred Qualifications:

 

BA/BA in Business, Finance or Economics, MBA a plus.

5 plus years of sales experience in financial services or leasing industries.

Strong Excel skills (ability to develop/communicate Excel based financial sales models).

Strong presentation skills.

Knowledge of credit and financial analysis; vendor program development; and financial concepts.

Detailed Description
OFD Salespeople will work closely with customers and Oracle license sales reps to structure payment solutions for acquiring Oracle and partner technology. This function includes: soliciting end - user payment transactions; training Oracle and partner license sales personnel to identify opportunities for accelerating technology sales through OFD engagement; participating with license sales in forecast reviews; advising customers of acquisition payment options, and generating proposals for payment solutions; obtaining credit approvals, providing financial and transaction analysis to assist credit underwriting; generating and negotiating payment contracts (within OFD business practices); obtaining customer signatures and assisting the Oracle contract review and signing process.
Job Requirements
BA/BA in Business, Finance or Economics. 2+ of sales experience, Presentation skills familiarity with credit, financial analysis and financial concepts. Familiarity with financial pricing.
