Customer Support Rep – Online Chat and Voice

Description & Responsibilities

•     In SaaS (Software as a Service) offering, a live trial is critical to the sales process, and helping new users get comfortable and productive is critical to retention. The difference between success and failure is the user’s ability to quickly become comfortable with the software and use it to perform the critical processes of their job. That’s where you come in!

•     A Customer Support Rep makes sure that users have the best possible experience with Oracle’s SaaS offerings, transition smoothly from trial to purchase, and continue to have a positive experience in daily use on the job.

•     This position focuses on providing help at the point where users need it most – right from within the software. Representatives will provide technical support from directly within the SaaS web interface via live online chat and voice.

 

Qualifications

•     College degree or equivalent work experience

  • Customer service focus, with a drive to own the customer’s problem and move it through to a solution

  • 2-3 years of experience supporting or working with one or more of the following:

    • Enterprise software such as Oracle e-Business Suite, PeopleSoft, JD Edwards, Seibel CRM, SAP

    • SaaS solutions such as SalesForce.com, Workday, NetSuite, etc.

    • Databases, middleware, or web technologies such as Java

  • Strong written communication skills

•       Domain knowledge of SaaS and Platform as a Service (PaaS) offerings a plus
