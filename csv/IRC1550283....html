Department Description
Fusion Territory Management is a key product in Fusion CRM product suite. It provides sales managers or territory administrators a tool to create optimized territories defined in terms of geographic or market segments, product or product lines, size of customer, or by specific customers or prospects. These territories serve as a basis for forecasting, quota, compensation, and analysis of sales performance and help provide a higher level of service. Optimized territories will provide representatives more time to follow-up on new business opportunities by reducing travel time, creating more revenue per coverage area.Fusion Territory Management is being built on Oracle Fusion middleware technologies, which includes ADF, ADF UI, SOA, Enterprise Scheduler, Essbase and BI.
