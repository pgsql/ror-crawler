Position: Service Delivery Manager (SDM)

The Enterprise Support Delivery team is looking for a highy motivated Service Delivery Manager to manage one of Oracle's top customers. Prefered candidates will posess excellent communication skills plus at least 4 years experience within Oracle Support or Consulting, directly supporting E-Business Suite applications and/or implementations.  An understanding of Value Chain Planning, Transportation Management and SOA/BPEL is a definite plus.  The ability to effectively interface with varying levels of customer contacts, a broad knowledge of support escalation process, along with supporting Oracle Applications implementations is desired.

Contact: John.Bechtel@oracle.com
 
Brief Description
As a member of the Support organization, your focus is to deliver post-sales support and solutions to the Oracle customer base while serving as an advocate for customer needs. This involves resolving post-sales non-technical customer inquiries via phone and electronic means, as well as, technical questions regarding the use of and troubleshooting for our Electronic Support Services. A primary point of contact for customers, you are responsible for facilitating customer relationships with Support and providing advice and assistance to internal Oracle employees on diverse customer situations and escalated issues.
Detailed Description
In this role, you will be considered the lead customer liaison for 4 -10 strategic, top-revenue Support accounts, and will attend one-on-one meetings with representatives of the strategic accounts. You will be responsible for managing internal projects, such as creating internal training curricula and schedules, presenting across lines of businesses, identifying and driving productivity improvement opportunities, coordinating, preparing and qualifying Premier Analysts for Customer Visits and employee mentoring.
Job Requirements
Leading contributor individually and as a team member, providing direction and mentoring to others. Work is non-routine and very complex, involving the application of advanced technical/business skills in area of specialization.
Additional Details

The customer you would be supporting is based in the US Eastern time zone.  They are currently in the process of implementing a new E-Business Suite R12 environment (primarily Financials, OM and Discrete Manufacturing) along side of a new Value Chaing Planning instance and a Transportation Management system.

The position allows you to work from your home office or a local Oracle office.

Typical deliverables include but are not limited to:

  • SR and Bug reporting and tracking (utilizing the internal Oracle CMS system)
  • Assistance with problem workarounds for open Service Requests
  • Assistance with the management, application and verification of Oracle patches
  • Conduct quarterly account reviews with Business Project Leader and and their staff
  • Participate in IT planning sessions
  • Escalation management for all Critical SR's
  • Engaging other Support, Development, Consutling and/or Sales resources as needed.
  • Upgrade and implementation proactive planning
  • Service Request (SR) review and trend analysis
  • Provide proactive recommendations designed to minimize future SR’s
  • Establish appropriate procedures and guidelines to pursue, track, and document service requests
  • Monitor service requests (SR’s) and communicate status to customer designated technical support contacts
  • Assist in developing and maintaining system documentation related to customer’s hardware and software configuration
  • Preparation of weekly status report for customer technical contact
  • On-site support during major technical upgrades
