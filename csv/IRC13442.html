Job Summary

The Front-end Collections Supervisors’ purpose is to lead a team of 15 full time and part-time collections representatives on our automated dialing system to exceed all collection goals.  A Collection Supervisor must be able to coach, mentor, motivate, and effectively communicate changes to their team as well as have the ability to identify process needs and suggest process improvements.  By overseeing and assisting collection representatives to exceed front-end collection goals, the Supervisor directly impacts Credit Acceptance profitability.
