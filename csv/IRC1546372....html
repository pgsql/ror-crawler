Position Overview:

As a member of Oracle's finance organization, you will be responsible for providing all aspects of financial support for planning and control. Assist in consolidation and reporting of financial results; preparation of annual budget. Gather, model, analyze, prepare, and summarize information for financial plans, operating forecasts, trended future needs, etc.; on a weekly, monthly, or quarterly basis. Prepares recommendations based on analyzes. May perform revenue and expense analysis, headcount and headcount driven expense analysis at cost center level, tracking of product and product line revenue, reconciliation of accounts receivable, variance analysis and ad-hoc analysis as needed. You may participate in cross-functional programs and projects.

 

Responsibilities:

Specific responsibilities include the following:

  • Forecasting:

    • Participate in forecast meetings for US ensuring understanding of potential risk/upside.

    • Work closely with other finance teams, sales, service, and operations in assisting toward execution of budget and forecast commitments, including communication of progress toward attainment and plans to effect course corrections as necessary.

  • Reporting and Analysis: Work with both finance and business management to identify and track appropriate performance measures.  Be proactive in reviewing information identifying inconsistencies, trends and issues for finance and business management.

  • Prepare financial information and coordinate the gathering of supporting operational data for monthly and quarterly reporting requirements. 

  • Budgeting – assist with generating budget models for the North America License Group.

  • Monitor accounting activities to ensure that financials are an accurate representation of results

·         Close – ensure that all expected revenue and expense is recorded on the books in the correct time frame for the monthly and quarterly closes.

·         Support both the people and the projects that reside in the North America License line of business.  The Sr. Financial Analyst will be expected to navigate through the various departments within the company to help resolve specific issues.  He/she will be responsible for performing the typical FP&A responsibilities of forecasting, budgeting, reporting as well as deal with ad hoc requests.

  • Leading contributor individually and as a team member, providing direction and mentoring to others

  • Work is non-routine and very complex, involving the application of advanced technical/business skills in area of specialization

  • Strong Microsoft Excel skills and proficiency in Oracle Financials and Business Intelligence Software (Oracle, Cognos, Siebel etc..)

  • Strong analysis and research skills

  • Ability to partner across functions

  • 5-8+ years relevant work experience

  • BS/BA in Accounting/Finance preferred

  • CPA/MBA desired.
