Department Description

The Exalogic Platform Team delivers the Exalogic Platform which includes the Exalogic rack and all the components within it (Compute Nodes, Storage Appliance, Infiniband Networking Components, Operating systems, tools, installers, firmware, etc). We integrate these components and develop original installers that image custom software stacks on to the compute nodes and original tools that configure the compute nodes and that configure all of the components to work together and to work within the customer's data center. We also develop components of the Exalogic Management Stack.   This team has diverse interactions with development and QA teams across many organizations in Oracle, including Systems (hardware) development.   Team members bring a systems oriented approach to the product development lifecycle.
Brief Description
Design, develop, troubleshoot and debug software programs for databases, applications, tools, networks etc.
Detailed Description
As a member of the software engineering division, you will assist in defining and developing software for tasks associated with the developing, debugging or designing of software applications or operating systems. Provide technical leadership to other software developers. Specify, design and implement modest changes to existing software architecture to meet changing needs.
Job Requirements
Duties and tasks are varied and complex needing independent judgment. Fully competent in own area of expertise. May have project lead role and or supervise lower level personnel. Preferred Qualifications: BS or MS degree or equivalent experience relevant to functional area. 4 years of software engineering or related experience.
