Detailed Description

This is a varied role comprising of shipping and warehousing responsibilities with quality and data analysis elements. The individual will be required to perform all daily shipping activities required by the site. This will include shipping non component shipments as well as receiving and processing all on site deliveries in a timely and efficient manner. The individual will also perform customer complaint investigation and analysis for European customers and report out on findings to the quality group. They will also be required to create and perform Risk Assessments with responsibility for ensuring Aspect and Impact requirements are met in line with ISO standards as well as management of the internal Recycling Programme. There is also an expectation that the individual will drive continuous improvement activities such as 5S throughout the site as well as assist with cost reduction efforts.

-       Involvement in all continuous improvement activities and drives throughout the site

-       Perform any other duties that may be asked of you
