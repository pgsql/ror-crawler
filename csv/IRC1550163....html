Department Description

The Integrated Marketing Campaigns Team's objective is to generate Demand for Oracle Primavera Enterprise Project Portfolio Management via an integrated global marketing program including events, online advertising, e-Marketing, Transactional campaigns and Social Media.
Brief Description
Development of communication programs to promote Oracle*s products & services.
Detailed Description
Integrate advertising into the marketing campaigns, exercising judgment and business acumen in selecting methods and techniques for best practice / creative solution. As a recognized authority and leading contributor maintain regular interface between the division and Corporate. Plan and manage pan-geography corporate and local campaign media plans. Plan, manage and implement corporate and local campaign advertising creative. Manage tier 2 local advertising and arrange sign off with Corporate. Integrate all advertising across the communications mix. Maintain and communicate brand advertising guidelines. Oversee partner advertising. Develop and manage brand communications audit. Measure advertising effectiveness.
Job Requirements
Leading contributor individually and as a team member, providing direction and mentoring to others. Work is non-routine and very complex, involving the application of advanced technical/business skills in area of specialization. 8 years relevant work experience. A proven track record in advertising across line-of-business and/or geography as an account manager, with experience of creative production and media buying.
Additional Details

Summary:

Responsible for development and execution of demand generation campaigns for Oracle’s Primavera Enterprise Project Portfolio Management.

  • Develop and execute integrated marketing programs for North America and Latin America, including events, e-marketing campaigns, online & search advertising, social media in support of Sales Revenue Targets

  • Collaborate closely with International marketing team and other Oracle business units in the coordination of worldwide and cross-selling marketing campaigns
