Department Description

This position is in the Application Development Information Technology Department, Supply Chain

Management (SCM) Group. The group is responsible for Testing, Release, & Environment

Automation Management. We provide automated and manual application/ environment setup services for the SCM programs.

These services include:

· Performing manual application setups

· Development and execution of automated application setup processes using Oracle’s iSetup, Application

Testing Suite, and other scripting methods

The individual will perform scripting tasks as well as executing automated and manual setups. They

will work with multiple teams across the department to test applications as we enhance and

maintain the business solution. This will be an exciting and challenging role with time-sensitive activities
Brief Description
Work with Oracle*s world class technology to develop, implement, and support Oracle*s global infrastructure.
Detailed Description
As a member of the IT organization, assist with the analyze of existing complex programs and formulate logic for new complex internal systems. Prepare flowcharting, perform coding, and test/debug programs. Develop conversion and system implementation plans. Recommend changes to development, maintenance, and system standards.
Job Requirements
Job duties are varied and complex utilizing independent judgment. May have project lead role. BS or equivalent experience in programming on enterprise or department servers or systems.
Additional Details

Candidate should have experience in the software setup and automation scripting fields.

Knowledge of automated set up processes and procedures is highly desirable, including experience with Oracle iSetup

Experience in Oracle Application Testing Suite, or other commercial automated scripting programs is desirable.

Knowledge of the Oracle eBusiness suite Supply Chain Management and Field Service modules is desirable.

In depth knowledge of complex spreadsheets for setup data management desirable.

The position will include co-ordination of testing activities within the group and with other department organizations.
