About this Position: The purpose of the Risk Analyst position is to . . .

§   Performs sophisticated analysis on portfolio performance, pricing and policy, operational effectiveness, etc.

§   Documents analysis and condenses conclusions for executive management as well as for peer analysts

Position Outcomes & Activities: You will be measured on your ability to perform the following activities effectively

§   Pricing - Maintain an advance formula that maximizes returns based on available capital by monitoring the origination systems and the performance of the business.

§   Forecasting - Provide accurate and timely forecasts (accuracy by segment as well as in aggregate) by monitoring performance.  Communicate the results consistently and clearly to appropriate stakeholders

§   Scorecard - Maintain origination scorecards that are accurate in total and by segment by monitoring the origination systems and the performance of the business.

§   Reporting - Provide reoccurring and ad-hoc reporting which gives us the necessary insight into all aspects of the origination process.  Undertake initiatives which allow us to address key business issues.
