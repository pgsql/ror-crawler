Job Title Program Manager 3- ProdDev
Location SANTA CLARA
Organization Name Systems Software Program Management
Department Description
Provide Program and Release Management services for all Systems SPARC and Solaris product releases
Brief Description
Manage the development and implementation process of a specific company product.
Detailed Description
Manage the development and implementation process of a specific company product involving departmental or cross-functional teams focused on the delivery of new or existing products. Plan and direct schedules and monitor budget/spending. Monitor the project from initiation through delivery. Organize the interdepartmental activities ensuring completion of the project/product on schedule and within budget constraints. Assign and monitor work of systems analysis and program staff, providing technical support and direction.
Job Requirements
Duties and tasks are varied and complex, needing independent judgment. Fully competent in own area of expertise. May have project lead role and or supervise lower level personnel. BS degree or equivalent experience relevant to functional area. 5 years of project management, product design or related experience preferred.
Additional Details
Manage and drive software and system product planning, development, and release activities to meet program commitments for feature content, quality criteria, and schedule. Apply expert product knowledge and project/program management skills in leading, driving, and supporting cross-functional teams of engineers, managers, and representatives from marketing, services, pubs, manufacturing. Track schedule and dependencies, manage risk, and drive issues to resolution. Ensure coordination among contributing organizations. Provide status and review at all levels. Ensure effective communication among groups and individuals. Actively contribute to management improvement and establishment of best practices. Handle multiple assignments.
