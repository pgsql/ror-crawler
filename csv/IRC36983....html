Company Overview

In 1983, the first online trade was placed using early E*TRADE technology.  Today, E*TRADE FINANCIAL is a global financial leader, delivering value and innovation to millions of customers in more than 40 countries worldwide.

 

We are looking for highly energized, dynamic professionals who are ready to join a fast-paced, world leader in the financial services industry. We move at the speed of the Internet.  Our work culture fosters an incredible exposure to innovative ideas and challenges that create experience and opportunity.  Many have risen through the organization to become industry leaders, in both the financial services world and in the community at large.  E*TRADE is made up of some of the brightest and most talented people in the industry - are you ready to join the industry leader?
Detailed Description
  • Retain assets of assigned affluent / wealthy households with significant holdings at E*TRADE FINANCIAL by effectively managing an assigned client base and ensuring that all clients receive a superb client experience.

  • Strengthen the client relationship and expand existing client wallet share through effective presentation of the investment products and services E*TRADE offers.

  • Proactive outbound calling to the existing E*TRADE FINANCIAL customer base to drive asset acquisition through customer account consolidation; maintain and update prospect and sales reports accurately.

  • Evaluate your client’s financial situation and identify investment objectives through a comprehensive and customized financial planning process; and provide advice guidance and execution on a variety of investment products and services such as Conservative Investment and Managed Investment Portfolio product offerings.

  • Cultivate current and prospective client relationships and identify opportunities to expand these relationships by responding to sales opportunities with creativity and innovation.

  • May perform additional duties as directed by management.

  • Strong history of success in building client relationships and client retention experience
  • Capable of quickly building good rapport with clients, assessing their needs and presenting features and benefits of E*TRADE products and services

  • The ability to multi task and work independently with a high degree of quality

  • Excellent communication and organizational skills
