Department Description
We are looking for one outstanding engineer with proven source code level defect resolution skills who wants to help us deliver top quality embedded systems software solutions to our end customers. We are a global customer facing software sustaining and new feature development organization offering the right candidate a great opportunity to work on amazing software products. We ask our engineers to draw from their technical breadth and depth of experience to analyze then root cause complex software product defects. If you desire a challenge, we have plenty of them for you!

Job Locations: Santa Clara, CA, San Diego, CA or Burlington, MA
Brief Description
Design, develop, troubleshoot and debug software programs for databases, applications, tools, networks etc.
Detailed Description
As a member of the software engineering division, you will assist in defining and developing software for tasks associated with the developing, debugging or designing of software applications or operating systems. Provide technical leadership to other software developers. Specify, design and implement modest changes to existing software architecture to meet changing needs.
Job Requirements
Duties and tasks are varied and complex needing independent judgment. Fully competent in own area of expertise. May have project lead role and or supervise lower level personnel. BS or MS degree or equivalent experience relevant to functional area. 4 years of software engineering or related experience.
Additional Details
Experience or understanding of the following is preferred:

 * BS or MS in CS or equivalent employment experience
 * Experience in firmware/embedded software development. Must have good understanding of embedded programming concepts:
      Thread control & management, resource sharing management (synchronization, mutex, and deadlock avoidance),
      interprocess communication, and interrupt processing
 * Strong development experience using C/C++, Java, Forth, and scripting languages in Unix environment
 * Strong background in computer systems architecture and related work experience
 * Knowledge in operating system internal such as Linux and Solaris
 * Very strong troubleshooting and debugging skills
 * Familiarity with assembly language programming is a plus
 * Self motivated, comfortable working independently as well as part of a team
 * 5 or more years professional experience is required
 * Outstanding teamwork, communication, and time management skills
 * Demonstrated PASSION to work on a team that delivers results on world-class products in a fast-paced environment
