Department Description
Oracle Education Initiatives reach 1.5 million students in 95 countries through two award-winning programs: ThinkQuest and the Oracle Academy. The Development group manages customer-facing and internal websites and systems for these programs. We are looking for a software developer to:
  1. Design and develop innovative, scalable solutions that enable wide program adoption.
  2. Work collaboratively with operations and marketing teams to ensure system design meets the needs of internal and external stakeholders.
  3. Assist in system planning, scheduling and implementation.
  4. Build and execute unit test plans.
This position will report to the Technical Director, Corporate Citizenship. It is a rare opportunity to deliver the power of Oracle technology to students and educators around the world.
Brief Description
Design, develop, troubleshoot and debug software programs for databases, applications, tools, networks etc.
Detailed Description
As a member of the software engineering division, you will assist in defining and developing software for tasks associated with the developing, debugging or designing of software applications or operating systems. Provide technical leadership to other software developers. Specify, design and implement modest changes to existing software architecture to meet changing needs.
Job Requirements
Duties and tasks are varied and complex needing independent judgment. Fully competent in own area of expertise. May have project lead role and or supervise lower level personnel. BS or MS degree or equivalent experience relevant to functional area. 4 years of software engineering or related experience.
Additional Details
Preferred experience/skills:
  • 3 -- 5 years of development experience.
  • Strong knowledge of PHP, Java, J2EE, SQL, Oracle database, HTML/CSS/JavaScript, localization, and dependable software engineering practices.
  • Excellent coding, debugging, and performance tuning skills in SQL, PL/SQL.
  • Experience with Linux, Apache, MySQL, Netbeans, Eclipse, and other open source components and PaaS environments.
  • B.S. degree in Computer Science or related field.
