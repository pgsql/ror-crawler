Detailed Description
In this role, you will join the Xilinx CTO Office (Research Labs) in Dublin (Ireland). You will work closely with the senior researchers within the team to create/evaluate novel ideas and implement prototypes on leading-edge FPGA's. The specific activities can be diverse ranging from analyzing case studies, developing new tools, writing application example, to presenting results and writing reports, publishing papers or submitting patent applications as appropriate.
Job Requirements
  • PhD/MS student in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering or related field
  • Familiar with HDL and C/C++. 
  • Expertise in system design (processor or FPGA based) and tool flow
  • Flexibility, interest in technology, eagerness to learn, and a good attitude towards work
  • Beneficial would be application domain background in one or more of the following areas: High-Level Synthesis for FPGAs, Digital Signal Processing (DSP) image processing, embedded processors architectures and embedded software developments tools and techniques.
