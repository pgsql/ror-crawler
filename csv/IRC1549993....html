Department Description

  ATG-CPQ has a unique approach for quality testing compared to local QA teams and development unit testing.

  Apart from executing development test plans and RTs in integrated environments, CPQ aims at simulating real time testing scenarios and complex situations for testing the applications.

  Real time in the sense, we try to create the environments to reflect client environments and perform quality testing from the client’s perspective to test the product integration and key functionalities.

  Creation of customer like environments may require us to create some customizations to fit into the system and test the products.

Brief Description
Responsible for developing, applying and maintaining quality standards for company products with adherence to both internal and external standards. Develops and executes software test plans. Analyzes and writes test standards and procedures. Maintains documentation of test results. Analyzes test results and recommends corrective actions.
Detailed Description
As a member of the technical/process QA division, you will design, install and evaluate quality control methods and systems. Design continuing maintenance of product quality. Develop standards and procedures to provide quality guidance and methods.
Job Requirements
Duties and tasks are standard with some variation; displays understanding of roles, processes and procedures. Performs moderately complex problem solving with assistance and guidance in understanding and applying company policies and processes. Preferred Qualifications: BS degree or equivalent experience relevant to functional area. A of 1 year of software engineering or related experience.
