Department Description

The Strategic Process Owner Program Office will manage (in varying degrees) projects within the Source to Settle (S2S) area.   Source to Settle includes Accounts Payable, Expense Reporting, Procurement, Fixed Assets and other related feeder systems.  This may include strategic projects working with Global Process Owners (GPO) as well as more operational focused projects across the teams. 

This position will work directly with the Director of the Program Office with this project coordination.  This team will either a) own projects outright, b) act as a project lead while coordinating with the S2S GPO team, or c) act as an agent during the project lifecycle only representing the S2S GPO team.  Sample project scopes include -

  • Oracle business expansion into new territories needing participation/inputs from all Finance teams.
  • Business Assessment and Audit or SOX reviews.
  • S2S Budget tracking and forecasting.
  • S2S Key Performance Indicator tracking, reporting, and review.
  • Corporate mandated projects as they arise.
  • User Acceptance Testing compilation and reporting with AIT projects.
  • Finance and AIT project Roadmap management.
  • S2S related communications and website.
  • S2S related employee training initiatives.
  • S2S related Financial Policy review and updates.
  • Quarterly Operations Reviews and updates
Brief Description
Provides programs to improve operational efficiency, consistency, and compliance in support of the organization*s financial and tactical business objectives. Provides business practices and processes. Develops, communicates, and trains the organization on business practices and processes.
Detailed Description
Serve as a liaison with other divisions such as Finance, Contracts, HR, Legal, Shared Services, Accounts Receivable, Purchasing, and Risk Management in an effort to ensure accurate and timely transaction processing. Collect, input, verify, correct, and analyze data to measure key performance indicator actual versus business objectives. Provide updates to management regarding budget to actual, informing them of deviations and opportunities. Provide management with economic impact and compliance issues surrounding key business decisions and/or deals. Communicate Oracle Business Practices to the organization and monitor process and approvals for full compliance. Drive implementation of new processes and procedures.
Job Requirements
Work involves some problem solving with assistance and guidance in understanding and applying company policies and procedures. Attention to detail critical. Ability to collect, organize, and display data in spreadsheet format. Follow-through skills necessary to get information from internal and third parties and have data errors/omissions corrected. Relationship management skills strongly desired. Strong written and verbal communication skills to interact with management and possible clients desired. 0 - 2 years relevant work experience.
Additional Details

Source to Settle Strategy and Operations Analyst  – This position is an analyst role within the Source to Settle Strategy and Operations team.  This team is involved with policy and process over the functional areas of expense reporting, accounts payable, purchasing, and fixed assets.  Specific job role and tasks may range from the transactional level (expense audit, invoice processing, purchase order processing) to the project level.  Possible project involvement may include concepts such as KPI gathering and analysis; quality audit and reporting; AIT projects that require user acceptance testing; departmental policy and process reviews to identify opportunities to implement best practices.  The preferred qualifications for this role include; a BS/BA in Business or related field; project management experience; ability to deliver high quality results on time; strong communication skills, both written and verbal, and the ability to influence individuals up and down the organizational hierarchy as well as outside the lines of business; an ability to collect, organize and display data in spreadsheet format; ability to take initiative; demonstrate strong leadership, analytical and decision making skills;  an ability to identify opportunities and develop effective solutions; and an intermediate level of understanding of Payables, Procurement, Expenses and Assets.
