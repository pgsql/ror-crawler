Detailed Description
Develop, create, and modify general computer applications software or specialized utility programs following standard procedures. Analyze user needs and develop software solutions. Specifically, design and develop complex test cases and manual and automated software test applications. Implement automated test suites, while also maintaining documentation test results and recommending corrective action. Analyze tests, resolve errors, implement complex solutions, and perform functionality and regression testing. Ability to work independently from design phase to implementation phase of software solution. Need to collaborate with different development teams to architect software solutions. May need to interact with both internal and external customers to support and maintain software solutions. May provide technical leadership to a group.
Job Requirements
Employer will accept Bachelor’s degree or equivalent* in Computer Science, Engineering, Business, Math, Physics or related technical field, plus 4 years of work experience in job offered or 4 years of work experience in a computer-related occupation. Education or experience to include: 1. Four years of Java programming experience; 2. Web development experience utilizing HTML/DHTML, CSS, and Javascript; 3. Experience with Struts MVC web application framework programming; 4. Experience with Hibernate ORM programming; 5. Experience utilizing J2EE/Servlet/JSP; 6. Experience with PL/SQL, JMS, JSNI, ExtJS, and XML/XSL development. Experience may be gained concurrently. Any suitable combination of education, training, or experience is acceptable. *Employer will accept two years of experience in lieu of a bachelor’s degree. Location: New York, New York
