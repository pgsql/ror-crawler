Job Title Master Principal Sales Consultant

Organization Name Oracle On Demand
Department Description

Oracle On Demand has been an industry-leading offering for business software services delivered online for over 10 years and continues to be one of Oracle's fastest growing businesses.  Oracle offers the broadest portfolio of subscription and managed application offerings available to enable clients around the globe to accelerate their implementations, reduce their total cost of ownership and unlock the value of their investment in Oracle software and systems.  Customers get better business results with Oracle On Demand.

 

The GlobalSolutionsCenter (GSC) team enables sales to exceed bookings and revenue targets by creating compelling, profitable and timely On Demand solutions for those clients.
Brief Description
Provides direction and specialist knowledge in applying the technology/application to client business. Facilitation of customer product/application understanding through presentations demonstrations and benchmarks; provision of support throughout the sell.
Detailed Description
As a Master Principal Sales Consultant you will be responsible as the expert for formulating and leading presales technical / functional support activity to prospective clients and customers while ensuring customer satisfaction. Acts as a technical resource and mentor for less experienced Sales Consultants. Focuses on large or complex sales opportunities that need creative and complex solutions. Develops productivity tools and training for other Sales Consultants. Develops and delivers outstanding Oracle presentations and demonstrations. Leads any and all aspects of the technical sales process. Advises internal and external clients on overall architect solutions.
Job Requirements
Acknowledged authority within the Corporation.  Acts as a leader of large-scale company initiatives.  Viewed by peers as a leader and top contributor and by line management as a key business partner. 3 years vendor (sales and marketing) experience. 7 years business experience with relevant computer applications or database/tools. Ability to implement the most advanced product features. Thorough knowledge of system and application design. In depth knowledge of competitors. Demonstrated project management skills. Demonstrated excellent verbal and written communication skills: needs analysis, positioning, business justification, closing techniques. Ability to persuade others through presentations, demonstrations, and written communication. Ability to travel as needed. BA/BS degree or equivalent, advanced degree highly desirable.
Additional Details

A Senior Solution Manager (Master Sales Consultant) provides pre-sales solution support and works in collaboration with On Demand Sales, License Sales, and Oracle Consulting in selling the offerings included in the Oracle's On Demand Services portfolio, including Oracle Applications On Demand (E-Business Suite, PeopleSoft, Hyperion, OTM, Demantra, Siebel, JD Edwards & Retail, Fusion Applications), Oracle Technology On Demand, Oracle Enhanced Recovery Services and Lifecycle Management Services.  This role performs a specialized function as a regional POC for that region's Sales Management for the remote solution management team and operates as an extension of the GSC management team.

 

RESPONSIBILITIES

·        Provide direct sales support for Oracle On Demand Sales Representative & License Account Managers by conducting discovery sessions, building solutions leveraging Oracle's On Demand service portfolio, providing solution design/recommendation presentations & general Q/A support for prospective customers to meet or exceed sales targets on key deals for the region where SME-level support is required for high-impact interaction with clients.  Often works in concert with the Solution Manager assigned to deal.

·        Mentor and assist the assigned Solution Manager as needed to collect and analyze prospective customer environment data (current infrastructure, application products implemented, product customizations [technical & functional]), and assist in mapping business needs for the purpose of recommending solutions based on Oracle On Demand offerings.

·        Assist sales teams in assessing unique customer service and/or infrastructure needs and developing solutions that meet client needs while supporting Oracle’s business objectives.

·         Help negotiate terms of the final agreement with client for those items requiring On Demand Delivery approval, e.g. SLAs

·         Mentor and assist the assigned Solution Manager in handling difficult challenges with the solution approach during conversations with the client.

·         Collaborate with the managers of the Solution Management team to provide effective matrix management interaction on hiring talent, assigning resources to deals, and approving solutions.

·        Periodically assist in the delivery of technical training for external presentation to existing and prospective customers and internal presentation to employees.

·        Periodically participate in various projects to create and enhance sales tools, technical collateral, etc. in support of the broader sales team. 

·        Provide a deal/activity/projects report to manager on a weekly basis.

 

CAPABILITIES

·        Advanced consultative sales presentation and positioning skills

·        Advanced oral/written technical and interpersonal communication skills

·        Strong analytical problem solving skills

·        Ability to interact effectively with other technical and non-technical individuals and different levels internally and externally through development of relationships and constructive resolution of differences.

·        Results-oriented, focused and independent

·        Advanced ability to prioritize/manage multiple tasks and meet deadlines

·        Excellent organizational skills

·        In-depth knowledge of application architecture and/or functional capabilities of at least one of Oracle's EBS, PeopleSoft, Hyperion, JD Edwards or Siebel product lines as well as a solid foundation in Application Server and Database concepts

·        Familiarity with Linux and/or UNIX (Solaris) and Windows Server operating systems and integration approaches common in a distributed computing environment: web services, SOA, SQL*Net-based integration (including database links, ODBC, JDBC, OCI), XML and proprietary messaging technologies, and services/bp orchestration. 

·        Demonstrated understanding of the value chain concept

  • Knowledge of networking, backup and recovery, change management, and industry standards such as ITIL for enterprise application

  • Familiarity with various delivery and commercial models from Oracle and other vendors for application management services, managed services, cloud services and Software as a Service (SaaS)
