Department Description

In today’s competitive environment, the health of enterprise applications could determine a customer’s degree of success in meeting business objectives. Across packaged and custom-built applications, the mapping between applications and their underlying infrastructure is becoming more and more complex. This could lead to more-frequent failures and longer downtimes, resulting in increased costs and lost productivity. Oracle’s next generation of enterprise management systems has what it takes to give customers the competitive edge.

 

Oracle is embarking on a new fundamental approach to Systems Management that will set us apart in the space – a Top-Down Application Management approach.  This approach will allow Enterprise Manager to deliver an Application Centric view of the IT Universe while aligning IT with the business.   Enterprise Manager will be the most comprehensive and integrated suite of application and infrastructure management software.

 

We are seeking an articulate, technically driven, executive-level QA leader who has a successful track record in leading a large engineering QA organization.
Brief Description
Manage a team that designs, develops, troubleshoots and debugs software programs for databases, applications, tools, networks etc.
Detailed Description
As a Vice President of the software engineering division, you will apply your extensive knowledge of software architecture to manage software development tasks associated with developing, debugging or designing software applications, operating systems and databases according to current and future design specifications. Build enhancements within an existing software architecture and envision improvements to the architecture.
Job Requirements
Assists in the creation of division strategy and consults with senior management in providing direction to ensure growth and financial success. Ensures a consistent approach of organizational policies and procedures. Demonstrated leadership and people management skills. Strong communication skills, analytical skills, thorough understanding of product development. BS or MS degree or equivalent experience relevant to functional area. Successful track record in software engineering management or related experience.
