Department Description
Oracle Talent Management is a fast growing family of applications that is key to Oracle’s ability to outpace the competition.  Talent Management is an exciting development group with fast-paced uptake of new technology and tools. Using our applications, customers can find the best talent for the job, develop talent skills and competencies, evaluate performance and provide effective feedback, compensate fairly and retain, and lastly, effectively manage talent profile and seamlessly provide core information to other applications.

Our growing customer base pushes us to be the leader in our market with modular development of leading-edge Java web applications.  Our team has a senior technical staff including patent-holders and years of experience rapidly building out capabilities in innovative ways.

Fusion is the next-generation version of the Oracle Applications Suite. Join the Talent Management development team to help design and build this breakthrough release.
