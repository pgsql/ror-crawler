Department Description

 Oracle’s Siebel CRM On Demand is leading the revolution by delivering “Software as a Service” via Internet. It provides market-leading BI Solutions, marketing, sales, services and call-center capabilities in a security-rich and reliable hosted environment. These services are delivered over the web, incorporating cutting edge technologies, industry-leading practices, processes, and methodologies for deploying and managing applications.

We are looking for highly energetic individuals to be a part of the global hosting team which deploys, manages and provides the mission critical support 24x7 to the customers. This team builds, configures and supports high performance, scalable, web and database server components, for the Oracle CRM OnDemand product (www.crmondemand.com). 
Brief Description
Work with a world class team to provide enterprise-wide, Oracle Database Administration support for production systems and provide DBA services to application development teams, including database design, database generation, coding, and database production support. Provide DBA support with a high degree of customer service, technical expertise, and timeliness. Provide accurate and creative solutions to user problems of moderate nature to ensure user productivity.
Detailed Description
Provide Development and/or Production support. This includes creating primary database storage structures, designing and creating primary objects, modifying database structure as necessary for enhancements or performance, creating documentation, designing interfaces, database objects, and creating plans. Monitor and optimize performance of the database, plan for backup and recovery of the database, and allocate system storage and plan future storage needs. Create database-related scripts and programs to support development and production environments. Research, evaluate, design, test, recommend, and plan implementation of new or improved RDBMS, ETL and database tools.
Job Requirements
Job duties are varied and complex utilizing independent judgment. May have project lead role. Preferred Qualifications: 5 years of experience supporting relational databases as a DBA, with multiple distributed relational DBSM's or a depth and focus in Oracle and related tools. Exposure and practice at using the DBMS to optimize performance of large databases for enterprise use is preferred. Experience with an organization with a key 24 X 7 reliance on its database is desirable. BS or equivalent desired.

Builds, configures, supports and ensures 24x7 availability of all production and stage environments.
