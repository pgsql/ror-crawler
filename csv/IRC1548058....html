Organization Name MySQL Enterprise Tools
Department Description
The MySQL Enterprise Tools team at Oracle delivers world-class, commercial tools to help our customers monitor, manage and performance tune their MySQL database servers.

You're joining a team that is committed to high software quality, a good user experience, and a healthy team culture. We're spread across many countries and time zones, but we work closely together via email, phone and electronic means. QA is integrated into our development team and process, and you will also interact directly with Support, Product Documentation and our partners in Product Management and Product Marketing. The position also includes occasional customer support either through discussions with our Support colleagues or through interactions directly with customers.
Brief Description
Design, develop, troubleshoot and debug software programs for databases, applications, tools, networks etc.
Detailed Description
As a member of the software engineering division, you will assist in defining and developing software for tasks associated with the developing, debugging or designing of software applications or operating systems. Provide technical leadership to other software developers. Specify, design and implement modest changes to existing software architecture to meet changing needs.
Job Requirements
Duties and tasks are varied and complex needing independent judgment. Fully competent in own area of expertise. May have project lead role and or supervise lower level personnel. BS or MS degree or equivalent experience relevant to functional area. 4 years of software engineering or related experience.
Additional Details
As a Software Developer, you will help deliver the MySQL Enterprise Monitor and related commercial products to customers across a variety of operating systems. Development is done in Java and uses Tomcat, Spring, Hibernate, MySQL and related technologies. The team is heavily invested in automated testing, continuous integration and other processes that support our high standards for quality and productivity. Use and maintenance of these and related tools is a requirement.

In this position, you will routinely act independently while working within established priorities and deadlines; you will often be required to document problems and solutions; you will routinely find, fix and document bugs; you will routinely review the work of colleagues, provide constructive feedback and respond constructively when they provide feedback to you.
