Additional Details

The storage appliance performance  Engineering team within Oracle’s Core OS & Storage organization is seeking performance benchmark and development engineers with a passion for solving complex problems.  Being able to accurately and reliably measure, report, and improve the performance of our storage products is a crucial part of developing and marketing our products.  He/She will be responsible for ZFS Storage Appliance performance measurement and analysis.     

 

Responsibilities:

* Lead a highly skilled engineering team to design and develop the Performance benchmark strategy, methodologies, tools infrastructure and efficient execution of the Performance strategy.

* Lead performance analysis and debugging effort

* Reviewer/advisor for system appliance-level performance strategies, data analysis, and results presentation

* Work with cross functional teams to analyze and enhance ZFS file system and various network protocols performance, such as NFS/iSCSI/FC/IPoverIB ..

* Serve as the Performance Engineering team's liaison with:

    - Capacity Planners

    - System Architects

    - Software Architects

    - Database Administrators

and find resolutions to some of the most complex problems in the storage area.
