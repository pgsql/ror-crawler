Department Description
Brief Description
As member of the Consulting Organization*s Sales team, you will be responsible for generating leads and closing deals selling Oracle Consulting Services. This will depend not only on your ability to develop strong relationships with client executives but also across Oracle. You will be responsible for qualifying and developing value add propositions for the customer.
Detailed Description
Position is responsible for selling consulting and/or outsourcing servicing. Client contact is typically with executive and senior executive level. Able to network and develop strong business relationships. Responsible for generating leads and submitting proposals to the client utilizing a broad knowledge of Oracle*s services. Primary job function is to nurture and close the sale.
Job Requirements
Assists in the development of short, medium, and long term plans to achieve strategic objectives. Regularly interacts across functional areas with senior management or executives to ensure unit objectives are met. Bachelor's degree with 7-10 years of direct sales experience. Industry experience and an ability to develop executive level relationships. Strong communication and negotiation skills. Willing to travel regionally.
