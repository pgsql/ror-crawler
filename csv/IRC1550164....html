Department Description
Quality Assurance Department at ATG-Oracle. ATG-Oracle delivers e-commerce capabilities to the world's best brands in a diverse range of industries
Brief Description
Responsible for developing, applying and maintaining quality standards for company products with adherence to both internal and external standards. Develops and executes software test plans. Analyzes and writes test standards and procedures. Maintains documentation of test results. Analyzes test results and recommends corrective actions.
Detailed Description
As a member of the technical/process QA division, you will design, install and evaluate quality control methods and systems. Design continuing maintenance of product quality. Develop standards and procedures to provide quality guidance and methods.
Job Requirements
Duties and tasks are standard with some variation; displays understanding of roles, processes and procedures. Performs moderately complex problem solving with assistance and guidance in understanding and applying company policies and processes. BS degree or equivalent experience relevant to functional area. A of 1 year of software engineering or related experience.
Additional Details
Reponsibilities: * Participate in the design and implementation of product software testing strategy for multi-tier enterprise applications in the area of electronic commerce; * Communicate with QA lead on testing progress, technical issues, risks, and schedule changes. * Review and provide feedback on product requirements, technical specifications, bug reports, install and configuration guides, testing artifacts and schedules. * Install and configure multi server production-like testing environments; * Implement automated tests and/or supporting utilities to assist in nightly product testing. This may include API as well as UI tests. * Work closely with development to resolve technical issues and to investigate customer issues that might not have been found on our testing environment. * Debug and support development in researching and resolving vendor issues. * Asses completeness, accuracy, usability, consistency of product under test Requirements: * 1-3 years software QA experience; * BS in CS or related field / experience; * Knowledge of various testing techniques, approaches and best practices; * Experience on test case authoring; * Experience with bug tracking, test case management systems, and source control systems * Experience designing and implementing automated system and unit level tests; * Experience programming on Java or other object oriented language. (Experience on JUnit, JTestcase, Cactus is a plus); * Experience writing UI automated scripts (Quick Test Professional, or Selenium experience is a plus); * Must be a strong team player, detail oriented, self-motivated and perform well under pressure; * Excellent communication skills, both verbal and written; * Excellent problem solving, troubleshooting, debugging and analytical skills; * Working knowledge on a variety of platforms including UNIX (Linux, Solaris and AIX) and Windows; * Experience in SQL and Relational Database, working knowledge in at least one major RDBMS vendor (MSSQL, Oracle, DB2, MySQL); * Experience with Application Servers (Weblogic, WebSphere or JBoss) is a plus.
