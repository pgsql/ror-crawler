Department Description

The charter of Oracle SSI - Managed Services team is to be a 'One Stop Support Partner' for its customers, providing support solutions to enable customers to get the best out of their Oracle software investment. To enable this, the Managed team offers a complete portfolio of services under the banner of Application Management Services (AMS). AMS aims at providing a single window for end-to-end managed services encompassing all the service components required to maintain a deployed application / infrastructure. AMS as a solution covers all of the following areas of service:

 

Ø  Hosting - Provisioning of managed development & test instances

Ø  Remote Monitoring - To remote monitor the applications infrastructure for availability & performance & provide associated incidence management services

Ø  Functional Help Desk - To assist users to resolve any application functionality issues

Ø  DBA Services - To provide reactive & proactive services to maintain DBA & Application stack

Ø  Tech Management - To provide reactive & proactive services to maintain Middleware stack

Ø  Software Maintenance - To maintain customizations to packaged products & maintain bespoke developed applications

Ø  Testing - Test automation of functional regression testing & load testing

Ø  Service Desk - To provide convenience of a single point of contact for all support issues & orchestrate all service delivery requirements
Brief Description
As a member of the Consulting organization, you are responsible for ensuring that a quality, integrated software solution is delivered in a timely manner, at budget, and to our client's satisfaction. This involves working closely with the client to understand and manage their expectations, as well as working closely with Oracle and third-party vendors to ensure delivery.
Detailed Description
Provide engagement delivery services both as an individual and as a team member. Services include identifying needs, developing, influencing and implementing proposals. Able to lead, support and participate on project teams.
Job Requirements
Job duties are varied and complex utilizing independent judgment. May have project lead role. A BS or BA in related fields. 7-9 years overall experience in functional or technical role. Two years experience in multi-dimensional issue resolution. Ability to communicate effectively. Ability to build rapport with team members and clients. Willing to travel regionally with some national travel.
