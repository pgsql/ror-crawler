Job Title FPGA Design Engineer
Location Saggart, IE
Organization Name Communications Design Center
Detailed Description

As a member of Xilinx Communication Design Center (CDC) the candidate will work as part of a team developing FPGA solutions for Wired and Wireless Telecommunication customers. This will involve implementing and verifying FPGA firmware or embedded software functions and integrating the functions into telecoms equipment such as wireless radio, baseband processing units, optical transport network or packet switches.

 

Xilinx is an international company and the successful candidate may occasionally be requested to travel.
Job Requirements

Required

  1. A primary engineering degree (or postgraduate) in electronics or equivalent

  2. Experience programming FPGAs using HDL (verilog or vhdl) design flows

Desirable

  1. Telecommunications systems knowledge

  2. Knowledge of standards such as Ethernet, SPI, 1588, AVB, OBSAI/CPRI, traffic managers or other Telecom standards

  3. Knowledge of Digital Signal Processing

  4. Scripting language experience like Perl or TCL

  5. Experience with C/C++, embedded processors, embedded operating systems
  6. Experience with Matlab for simulation of algorithms

  7. Experience with Modelsim simulation tools and Xilinx ISE tools

  8. A self-starter and team player

  9. Excellent interpersonal, written and verbal communication skills.
