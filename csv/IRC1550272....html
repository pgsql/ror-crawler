Job Title Database Administrator 3-IT
Location BANGALORE
Organization Name Product Development IT - ERP Services
Department Description
Product Development IT - ERP Services is responsible for providing secure, reliable, flexible and scalable infrastructure (database, middleware) to Oracle's internal Applications IT organization, in particular, for Oracle’s internal business ERP implementation called the Global Single Instance (GSI).   PDIT ERP works to ensure the operational integrity of these large business applications which are critical to Oracle's internal and external operations.
Brief Description
Work with a world class team to provide enterprise-wide, Oracle Database Administration support for production systems and provide DBA services to application development teams, including database design, database generation, coding, and database production support. Provide DBA support with a high degree of customer service, technical expertise, and timeliness. Provide accurate and creative solutions to user problems of moderate nature to ensure user productivity.
Detailed Description
Provide Development and/or Production support. This includes creating primary database storage structures, designing and creating primary objects, modifying database structure as necessary for enhancements or performance, creating documentation, designing interfaces, database objects, and creating plans. Monitor and optimize performance of the database, plan for backup and recovery of the database, and allocate system storage and plan future storage needs. Create database-related scripts and programs to support development and production environments. Research, evaluate, design, test, recommend, and plan implementation of new or improved RDBMS, ETL and database tools.
Job Requirements
Job duties are varied and complex utilizing independent judgment. May have project lead role. Preferred Qualifications: 5 years of experience supporting relational databases as a DBA, with multiple distributed relational DBSM's or a depth and focus in Oracle and related tools. Exposure and practice at using the DBMS to optimize performance of large databases for enterprise use is preferred. Experience with an organization with a key 24 X 7 reliance on its database is desirable. BS or equivalent desired.
Additional Details

The Product Development IT - ERP Services Team works alongside developers and support teams to ensure consistent response time and throughput for applications supporting a majority of Oracle's global business operations.  We provide services related to monitoring, proactive and reactive performance diagnosis and issue resolution, performance and capacity planning, and application and infrastructure reviews, to maximize the user's experience of Oracle technologies. Currently, we support a total of over 30 production environments and over 100 individual Oracle technologies, including Oracle's vast internal deployment of the Oracle E-Business Suite. This responsibility puts the members of this team in a unique position to consult with Oracle's Application and Server Technologies product development teams in identifying performance improvement opportunities in many of Oracle's most popular product suites, RDBMS, hardware, testing and monitoring tools.

The Product Development IT - ERP Services team is interested in individuals with the following skills/experience:

  • A proven track record in developing SQL and PL/SQL code.  Java, ADF, and APEX skills would be ideal, but not essential.
  • Strong performance tuning experience, including enabling and interpreting extended SQL tracing (event 10046).
  • Working knowledge of the Oracle Database's internal structures.  For example latches, enqueues, mutexes, and the Oracle Wait Interface.
  • An understanding of the Optimizer and how to influence it using SQL Hints, SQL Profiles, SQL Baselines, schema design, and database configuration.
  • An understanding of performance related features in versions 9i, 10g, and 11g of the Oracle RDBMS.
  • Experience in performing and analyzing the result of a performance test.
  • Strong problem solving abilities, a results-oriented nature, customer focused, and with good communication skills.
  • A self-starter with the ability to work independently, and as a member of a global team.
  • Hands on experience using Enterprise Manager to monitor for and investigate performance issues.
  • Knowledge of performance testing tools like Oracle Application Testing Suite, HP LoadRunner, Simora, etc. would be an advantage.
